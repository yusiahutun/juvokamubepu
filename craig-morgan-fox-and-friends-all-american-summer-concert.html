Craig Morgan to Headline Fox and Friends All-American Summer Concert Series in July

Craig Morgan's summer is getting busier and busier with each passing day. In addition to his hectic touring schedule, his daughter's recent wedding, and hosting the third season of his 'All Access Outdoors,' Morgan is now set to headline the 'Fox and Friends' All-American Summer concert series in New York City.

Morgan's performance in the Big Apple is set for Friday, July 13. It's then that he will perform his current Top 20 hit 'This Ole Boy,' as well as other fan favorites.

"That’s a big deal," Morgan tells Taste of Country. "It’s one of the most watched concerts series on a major network TV program, outside of the award shows. 'Fox and Friends' has a great viewership, so to be on that show is a big honor. I enjoy those people. We’ve been on 'Fox and Friends' quite a few times over the years, and they’re good people. They’re fun to work with, and I enjoy that. This is the first time we’ve done this particular summer concert series, and it’s a big deal. We’re real proud to be part of it."

Those in New York City can attend the free show when they film the concert outside the Fox News studios on Avenue of the Americas and 48th Street in the heart of Manhattan at 7:30AM that morning in July.

Just 12 days before the concert series, Morgan's third season of 'All Access Outdoors' will premiere on the Outdoors Channel (July 1).
