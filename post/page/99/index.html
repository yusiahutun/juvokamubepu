Muscatine compost facility now open

Apr 25, 2024 The compost facility at the Muscatine Transfer Station is now open. Muscatine and Fruitland residents can take yard waste compost facility free of charge. Residents can also bring brush and branches under 10 inches in diameter and 10 feet in length to the facility. Larger tree sections and logs can also be taken, but tree stumps or roots are not accepted. Residents are reminded that all loads need to be secured during transportation to and from the compost site to ensure loose material doesn’t fall onto city streets.
