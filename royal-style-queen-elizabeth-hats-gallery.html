The Queen's colourful hat collection! Her most stylish headpieces over the years

Her Majesty loves to accessorise with a vibrant hat

    The Queen knows how to choose a fabulous hat! For years we've been treated to a plethora of stunning headpieces from our wonderful Monarch Elizabeth - from vibrant pinks to sunny yellows and elegant blues. Her Majesty loves to adorn her hats with floral detail, adding that extra-special touch. The Queen certainly doesn't need to wear a crown to make her mark in the royal fashion world.

    The Queen looked so chic in this matching blue hat and dress coat at the 2019 Investec Derby Festival.

    Her Majesty wore this gorgeous pink hat with chiffon ruffle detail on 5 May 2019 as she met veterens during commemorations for the 75th Anniversary of the D-Day landings.

    A beret style in green matched the Queen's emerald brooch in this vintage shot. 

    Queen Elizabeth had a blue floral moment while waving from the balcony of the Broadway Theatre during a day of engagements in the London Borough of Barking and Dagenham. 

    Yes, those are velvet roses – and yes they looked perfect adorning Queen Elizabeth's hat during her visit to the RAF. 

    Duchess Kate took a nod from Queen Elizabeth and wore an elegant topper during the Diamond Jubilee in Nottingham. 

    Here we see the Queen looking stunning in bubblegum pink during a royal meet and greet in Kent, England. 

    We love this look! Queen Elizabeth sported polka dots from head to toe during her trip to Mexico in 1975. 

    Queen Elizabeth was a pink lady in a darling spring hat during a meeting with the staff at the Dover Ferry terminal in Dover, Kent. 

    Here's the Monarch elebrating her 67th wedding anniversary with a chic hat fit for a Queen. 

    Queen Elizabeth chose this pretty blue creation for the 70th Anniversary of VE Day at Westminster Abbey. 

    During a trip to the Westminster Abbey after its restoration, the ever-chic Queen matched her pink and green printed hat with her dress and coat. 

    Queen Elizabeth wore a mustard-coloured hat along with pearls and a Regimental brooch. Such a fashion-forward look for the Monarch! 

    The Queen wore a powder pink hat and jacket for great-granddaughter Princess Charlotte's christening. 

    The Queen was a vision in this vibrant red hat with a black brim during a Sunday service at the Church of St Peter & St Paul. 

    Queen Elizabeth opted for a black lace outfit by Maureen Rose and a pillbox hat with a veil designed by milliner Frederick Fox, during a visit to the Vatican. 

    Queen Elizabeth wore this bow-bedecked beauty during her visit to St. John's College at the University of Cambridge. 

    Queen Elizabeth was all smiles in a hat that featured trim matching her coat and Prince Philip's tie during the traditional Royal Maundy service at St George's Chapel, Windsor Castle. 

    This gray brimmed hat was situated perfectly at a jaunty angle as the monarch waved from aboard the royal yacht in Cape Town, South Africa. 

    We love this floral scarf so much we're giving it a place on our hat list! The Queen covered her curls in style while watching the Royal Windsor Horse Show. 

    Queen Elizabeth had a purple and black hat moment during her visit to the Southwark Cathedral. 

    Royal style in black and white as the Queen watched children play at the Devlin Castle Hotel in Slovakia. 

    Cheery yellow was the colour of the day during the opening of the Bexleyheath, Danson House. 

    The Queen's hat was turquoise and blue perfection during a trip to Jordan. 

    Queen Elizabeth's hat complimented the beige and blue accents on her dress during her visit to NYC for the September 11 wreath dedication ceremony. 

    Queen Elizabeth wore a fuchsia hat with red feather detail during a visit to the Lister Community School in Plaistow. 

    All white with a bow to match! Queen Elizabeth wore a white topper complete with a small bow during a visit to the Technical University. 

    Queen Elizabeth wore a wonderful velvet topper during her visit to the Urbis Center in Manchester, England. 

    There was a black adornment for contrast on this yellow creation during her visit to the Prince's Trust.
