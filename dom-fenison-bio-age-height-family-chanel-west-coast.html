Chanel West Coast is single no more after she went public with her relationship with her boyfriend Dom Fenison. They confirmed the relationship in February 2022. So you might wonder who is Dom? What is his age? What does he do to make a living?

This Dom Fenison Bio got everything covered. Keep on scrolling to learn more about him.

Meet Dom Fenison, Chanel West Coast Boyfriend

American TV personality rapper and singer Chanel West Coast has confirmed her relationship with her boyfriend Dom Fenison. She broke the news during an exclusive interview with In Touch at Sports Illustrated The Party x Palm Tree Crew. She told the outlet, “He’s a really popular model.”

On social media, Dom featured Chanel on 2 February 2022.

Chanel said the relationship between the two is new but they go way back. Dom shared, “We’ve known each other for, like, five or so years, but we kind of just rekindled our relationship ‘cause I was in her new music video for ‘Vinyl’.” His girlfriend dropped the song on Valentine’s Day.

The couple has been “formally” dating for a few months and is overjoyed! Chanel and Dom joked that they are “working on” becoming America’s “It” pair. Furthermore, the couple agrees that “a little bit of touch and words of encouragement” are their love languages.

Dom and Chanel raved over each other on social media in honor of Valentine’s Day. The MTV star captioned her Instagram Story with a cheerful snapshot with Dom, “My Valentine.” “Baby crushed it,” the Los Angeles-based model captioned excerpts of the “Vinyl” music video on his own Instagram Story.

Chanel’s music career is thriving, in addition to her developing romance! Fans may expect something different from the “No Plans” musician with a new album due out in “spring/early summer.”

Chanel explained, “The inspiration is what I go into the studio that day feeling. Some days I’m, like, depressed, so we’re writing a sad love song. Some days I’m happy writing a turn-up, it’s going to be a party song. I usually just go into the studio and make a song usually to go with what I’m feeling that day. So, the album is a culmination of all my different emotions over the past two years, which is a lot of emotions.”

Before Dom, Chanel had dated Scottish singer Liam Horne from April 2014 to July 2017. From July 2017 to October 2017, she linked romantically with rapper Solo Lucci. Although Dom’s previous relationships remain anonymous, he appeared with singer Hailee Steinfeld in the video of the song ‘Let Me Go’.
