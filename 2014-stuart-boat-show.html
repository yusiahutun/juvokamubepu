2014 Stuart Boat Show

Dr. Nick Iannotti and Stacey Emanuel

Hundreds came out to the 40th annual event, with more than 500 boats on display, from small bay boats up to huge 70-foot-plus sport fishermen. The show also featured nearly 170 exhibitors carrying engines, marine electronics and marine accessories from manufacturers worldwide. The event is the largest boat show on the Treasure Coast.
