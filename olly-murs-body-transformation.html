Olly Murs shows off his incredible seven-week body transformation!

The singer is dating bodybuilding champion Amelia Tank

    Olly Murs has taken to Instagram to unveil his amazing new physique. The singer shared two photos taken just seven weeks apart, as he spoke about the impact his weight loss has had on his wellbeing. "Ain't bad for a 35yr old!" Olly began. "My ambition to be a 'butler in the buff' is coming true!" The singer explained that the first picture was taken on the 2 January, and admitted that at the time he felt "grumpy", had "no energy" and that his "sleeping was awful". "So I made some adjustments," he concluded. "And I'm actually buzzing. Now I'm off to get a McDonald's!"

    Olly Murs has unveiled his incredible body transformation on Instagram

    No doubt Olly's new romance has something to do with his transformation. The former star is dating bodybuilding champion Amelia Tank. The couple confirmed their relationship on New Year's Day in a cute picture that Olly shared on his Instagram. He uploaded a picture of them both and captioned it: "2019 was a very positive year for me... winning The Voice with Molly, toured my album, had long overdue knee surgery, and met an amazing woman in the process!! 2020 I'm ready for ya! Love you all, and HAPPY NEW YEAR!!"
