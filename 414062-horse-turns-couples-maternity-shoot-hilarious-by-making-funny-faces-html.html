Horse Turns Couple's Maternity Shoot Hilarious by Making Funny Faces

  • Kristen Zaffiro recently landed on what can be termed as the photographer's lottery, thanks to a horse
  • During a maternity photoshoot, the couple's horse joined the set and started making funny faces
  • The photos have since gone viral as netizens laugh at the result of the memorable photoshoot

A would-be ordinary day for a maternity photoshoot turned hilarious when a cheeky horse jostled its way into the set and started making funny faces.

It all started when the clients requested photographer Kristen Zaffiro to include their horses in some of the photos.

After making the request, their horse Buckshot strolled into the set and posed, KSBW reported.

"I had a maternity photo session and the horse actually smiled when I told him to smile, then when I laughed, he laughed and really carried on," Zaffiro said.

In what she termed as the best maternity session ever, Zaffiro revealed that it was a challenge at first because the horses were not standing the way they had hoped they would.

"We finally got them turned around the correct way and I said, ‘At least you could smile.’ And he did,” she said, as reported by Kark.com.

The photos caused a stir online as many were left in stitches courtesy of the horse's antics.
