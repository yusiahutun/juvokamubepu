Chrissy Teigen Shares a Candid Nude Photo of Her and Baby Esti Taking a Bath

Chrissy Teigen is offering a candid glimpse into life with her newborn baby girl, Esti.

In a new Instagram post, the model shared a nude photo of herself and little Esti taking a bath together. Sitting in the middle of a bath tub filled with water, Teigen sits down with a small white towel spread over her lap and cradles her baby girl over the towel. Esti adorably looks up at her mother while Teigen smiles down.

"A boob that somehow hangs all along my side and deep purple lifetime scars but you are too perfect to worry about any of it," Teigen wrote in the caption.

Teigen and husband John Legend welcomed Esti's arrival in January 2023. The couple also share two other children: seven-year-old daughter Luna and four-year-old son Miles.

The model is known for providing an unfiltered perspective on her life as a mom.

She recently opened up about the struggles of C-section recovery shortly after giving birth to Esti. On Instagram, Teigen posted a photo of herself wearing a slipdress with stains located where she had lactated and where her C-section wounds are. "Omw to bandage together both my wound and 3.8 second clips of Beyonce performing in Dubai until I get a full hbo special," she captioned the photo.

After her pregnancy with Miles, Teigen also shared photos of her postpartum body on social media and prompted a conversation about the insecurities some might have after giving birth.

"I know how hard it can be to forget what (for lack of a better word) regular ol’ bodies look like when everyone looks bonkers amazing," she tweeted at the time. "Also I don’t really call this 'body confidence' because I’m not quite there yet. I’m still super insecure. I’m just happy that I can make anyone else out there feel better about themselves!"
