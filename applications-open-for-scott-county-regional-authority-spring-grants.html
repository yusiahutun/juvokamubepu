Applications open for Scott County Regional Authority spring grants

The Scott County Regional Authority is now accepting online submissions for the spring 2024 grant cycle.

Click here for an online grant application. Spring applications are for programs or projects that will be completed between June 1, 2024 and May 31, 2025. The deadline for online submission is 11:45 p.m. on Monday, April 1 and grants will be announced on Tuesday, May 21 at 1 p.m.

Any nonprofit organization that is exempt from federal income taxes under section 501(c)(3) or 501(c)(19) of the Internal Revenue Code, government organization or education institution can apply for a grant. Applicants must have a local office, a local corporate board of directors, be incorporated in the state of its principal place of business or chartered by the U. S. Government and serve residents of Scott County, Iowa.

Email SCRAIOWA@aol.com or call (563) 344-2626 with questions.
