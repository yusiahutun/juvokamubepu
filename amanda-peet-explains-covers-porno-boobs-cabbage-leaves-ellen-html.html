Amanda Peet explains why she covers her 'porno boobs' with cabbage leaves on Ellen

It might sound unusual - but using cabbage leaves to ease post-birth pains is a tried and trusted home remedy.

And Amanda Peet embraced the natural treatment ahead of her appearance on the Ellen DeGeneres Show.

Sharing a snap of herself with the chilled leaves stuffed into her bra, the 42-year-old explained how they are traditonally used to ease swollen breasts while breastfeeding.

The Togetherness star's appearance came just four weeks after giving birth to her third child, Henry.

'Apparently when you get engorged after the baby's born and the milk comes in you can put cold cabbage leaves on your boobs,' the A Lot Like Love actress said. 'Apparently it stops the milk.'

After Ellen shared photo proof of Amanda stuffing cabbage in her black bra, Amanda quipped: 'This is my husband being very amused by my porno boobs even though I'm in excruciating pain.'

Back to work: The 42-year-old actress appeared on the daytime talk show just four weeks after giving birth to her third child, son Henry

She and partner David Benioff, who is one of the creators of HBO's Game Of Thrones, welcomed their third child on December 6.

The married couple of eight years also have a seven-year-old son Frances and four-year-old daughter Molly together.

Amanda, who will be celebrating her birthday on January 11, admitted that she used the cabbage technique during her two other pregnancies but revealed that the odd remedy barely treats her pain.

'I've used it every time,' she continued to explain to Ellen. 'It doesn't really work really. It makes for a really great picture!'

The new mom has quickly rebounded back to work in order to promote her show Togetherness.

On Tuesday, Amanda was seen attending the Los Angeles premiere of the HBO comedy series at the Avalon in Hollywood looking elegant and bundled up.

She looked in incredible shape for just giving birth to a baby wearing a long cashmere coat over a shimmering silver and black dress.
