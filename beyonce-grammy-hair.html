Get The Grammys Look: Beyonces Glamorous Waves

Steve Granitz/WireImage

It’s no secret that we here at Daily Makeover are obsessed with all things Beyonce. After rocking shorter, blonder styles styles for the last year, Queen Bey shut the 2015 Grammys DOWN with her wavy, extra-long hair. We’re feeling this look so much that we consulted Pantene celebrity stylist Kim Kimble on how to get this look ourselves. Good news: it’s WAY easier than you think. Step 1: A clean canvas. Start with clean hair (newly washed or a day out) and focus on lightweight products that will add shine and minimize frizz without weighing hair down. Looking sleek doesn’t mean looking unwashed! Step 2: Volumize! Extra-long, center-parted hair can look flat–so added volume is key. A volumizing product like Pantene Pro-V Full & Strong PowerFULL Body Booster Spray all over hair. Not only will this make your hair look amazingly full and shiny, it will also help protect your lovely locks from the ravages of heat styling. If you’re prone to flyaways, add a small amount of anti-frizz serum to damp hair before blow-drying it straight. Step 3: Wave. Separate hair into two-inch sections and use a 1.5″ barrelled curling iron to create your glamorous, Beyonce-esque waves. Kimble has the inside scoop on the best (and easiest) waving technique: “Place the flat iron a few inches below the root and twist the flat iron a turn and a half before gliding the iron down the hair shaft,” she says. “This adds a curl throughout the entire length of hair, while also smoothing it out and making it look effortless.” Step 4: Loosen and straighten. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curl. If it’s looking TOO done, run a flat iron on low heat over each section of hair to flatten it out a little–but don’t overdo it. “You don’t want to make it too flat or straight, or else the wave will fall out,” says Kimble. Step 5: Shine on! Add some shine serum to your newly tousled waves to get them totally red carpet ready. Kimble loves Pantene Pro-V Smooth & Sleek Smooth Serum with Argan Oil because it’s super lightweight, never greasy and helps fight humidity. Step 6: Add hold. A medium-hold hairspray helps keep the style together without sacrificing movement. Pantene Pro-V Stylers Shaping Hairspray will give your waves outrageous hold, without making them crunchy.
