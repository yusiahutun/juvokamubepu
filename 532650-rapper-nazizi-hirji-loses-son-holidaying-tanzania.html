Rapper Nazizi Hirji's Son Dies While Holidaying in Tanzania

  • Veteran rapper Nazizi Hirji is mourning the passing of her secondborn son, Jazeel, which occurred on Christmas Day
  • Nazizi issued a statement revealing that her youngest son died after an unfortunate accident at a hotel in Tanzania
  • Nazizi's son was buried on December 27 in Nairobi per the Islamic religion, and her family asked for privacy as they process the loss

Rapper and Vybez Radio presenter Nazizi Hirji has announced the death of her son.

Nazizi Hirji's emotional post after losing son

The mother of two issued a statement revealing she lost her youngest son while holidaying in Tanzania.

In her statement, Nazizi wrote:

"It is with profound sorrow and deepest heartache that we confirm the tragic passing of Jazeel on Christmas Day, December 25, in Tanzania. We lost this young soul in an unfortunate accident at a hotel where the family was staying."

The little boy was buried on December 27 in Nairobi in accordance with the Islamic religion.

Nazizi urged the public to respect her time as she processed her loss.

"We appeal for your compassion and understanding out of respect for their need for peace in this time of mourning. We kindly request everyone to allow Nazizi and her family the space they need to begin the healing process," the statement read.

In May 2021, Nazizi introduced her son to the online community with a cute snap.
