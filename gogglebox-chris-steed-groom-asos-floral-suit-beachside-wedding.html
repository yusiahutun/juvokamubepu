Gogglebox's Chris Steed's groom rocked ASOS floral suit for beachside wedding

Stephen Webb's former Gogglebox partner got married in 2017

    Chris Steed has left behind his TV career to live in Wales with his husband Tony Butland, whom he married in Brighton on 8 June 2017.

    The Gogglebox star, who starred in the show in 2013 alongside Stephen Webb, is approaching his sixth wedding anniversary which has got us reminiscing about his beachside nuptials. In photos taken by Nick Ford and shared exclusively with HELLO!, Chris looked dapped in a bespoke Dolce and Gabbana aquamarine silk suit which he teamed with a white bow tie and a pink buttonhole, which tied in with his husband's wacky attire. 

    View post on Instagram 

    Instead of an understated blue or black suit, which are traditional colours for grooms, Tony opted for an eye-catching white suit covered with a red and purple floral print from ASOS. Adding more pops of colour, he teamed it with a red shirt and matching bow tie.

    We've tracked down the past season ASOS buy and the jacket retailed for a bargain £85 which went down to £42 in the sale, while the skinny trousers were reduced from £40 to £20.

    PHOTOS: Gogglebox stars' private wedding photos: From Pete's beachside nuptials to Mary's unconventional gown

    Sadly, neither is available to buy, but there are several alternatives in rust floral or sage green swirl prints for grooms looking for a statement outfit.

    Even the miniature figurines of Chris and Tony on top of their pastel pink wedding cake showed them in their colourful wedding suits!

    Reflecting on his emotional wedding day while on their mini-moon in Sorrento, Italy, Chris told HELLO!: "Tony always says I haven't had an emotion since 1983 and do you know what? When I saw the flowers... I started choking up. I got up to my room and I saw one of my best friends and he looked at me and was like, 'Are you alright?' and I was like, 'No!'

    "I've never cried like that... I usually have as much emotion as a brick and I was sobbing like a baby for most of the day!" 

    Tony added: "For me, it really took me by surprise that it touched my little prince that much that it drove him to emotion! I never ever expected him to see him like that about anything... but it was beautiful. It was."
