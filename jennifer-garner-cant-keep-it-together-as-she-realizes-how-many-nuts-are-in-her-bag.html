Jennifer Garner can't keep it together as she realizes how many nuts are in her bag

Jennifer Garner has treated fans with a classic “What’s in my bag” video on TikTok, but even she was surprised to see some of the contents.

The Hollywood star told her fans in the caption that she was embarrassed to even show them, but it seems like that didn’t stop her from releasing the hilarious video.

Jennifer Garner has endless amounts of nuts in her bag

Taking to her TikTok followers, the movie star sat on the floor while emptying the contents of her black backpack.

In her bright red dungarees, Garner started pulling out some staple (and pretty normal) items, including a notebook, glasses case, and pens.

However, towards the bottom of her bag, things started to get tastier, as she pulled out an array of candies, followed by a tiny bag of nuts.

She then proceeded to pull out more candy and even more nuts, and she couldn’t hold in her laughter as she made the endless discoveries.

At the end, she counted up all the bags of nuts and realized she had eight big bags of nuts hiding away in there. We know what she won’t be adding to her shopping list any time soon!

Fans couldn’t get enough of the video, as one penned: “All this video did was remind me of how much I love Jennifer Garner.”

“Ma’am , do you share your backpack with squirrels?” joked another.

The Elektra star wishes her ex-boyfriend a Happy Birthday

The TikTok video is not the 51-year-old’s only social media post that has gained attention lately.

Earlier today, the star wished her former boyfriend, and co-star Michael Vartan a happy 55th birthday on Instagram.

She shared a cute throwback snap of the two from the set of Alias, as she wore a traditional German costume.

In the caption, she wrote: “I can’t remember why I was Bavarian, but I do know today is your birthday. I hope it’s a great one, Michael! XX S. Bristow.”

The couple dated between 2003 and 2004, just before she started dating her former husband, Ben Affleck.
