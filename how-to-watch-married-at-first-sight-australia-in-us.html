The franchise, which started in 2013 in Denmark, follows three to 11 couples who are matched by a team of experts based on their backgrounds and personalities and marry upon the first time meeting each other. Instead, the couples take part in an unofficial commitment ceremony and decide if they want to stay together in the end or split.
