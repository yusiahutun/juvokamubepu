With her flair for the dramatic, Nicole Richie is the perfect person to play Morticia Addams.

The Fashion Star mentor and her family were deadpan in a Halloween photo that was posted on Richie's Instagram on Friday, where they display a creepy resemblance to the Addams Family.

Nicole, 32, was head-to-toe in black to portray the Gothic character, alongside husband Joel Madden and children Harlow, five, and Sparrow, four.

They're creepy and they're kooky! Nicole Richie, Joel Madden and their two children dress as the Addams Family for Halloween

Lionel Richie's daughter flawlessly pulled off the creepy matriarch with a long dark wig, sunglasses and flowing velvet dress in the photo that she captioned: 'The Madden Family.'

She completed the look with a vampish pose as she stood with her hand on one hip and bright red lips creating a sharp contrast to her pale skin.

Good Charlotte rocker Joel, 34, played the part of Gomez in a velvet suit and red neckerchief, if somewhat a little less convincingly.

Seriously creepy: The cast of the 1991 film The Addams Family, epitomised the kooky clan

Brightly dress: Nicole and her family (pictured here at the Disney Princess Court event in London on October 2) are normally seen in more colourful attire

Meanwhile, Harlow was adorable as Wednesday - the Addams family daughter who was most recently played by Christina Ricci in the 1991 film.

With her long hair in plaits and a black school dress and pink shoes, the little girl had an ironic smug expression on her face.

Meanwhile younger brother Sparrow looked sweet and innocent as Pugsley, wearing a cute black outfit.

Richie really did get in the spirit of things this Halloween, as on Wednesday she posted a photo with her girlfriends at the Haunted Hayride, where they were all laughing in delight.

Nature day: Nicole and friend Cara Santana hiked Runyon Canyon in Los Angeles on Wednesday

Fresh air and good friends: The girls enjoyed a catch up on the trail with their dogs

The former Simple Life star got back to basics during the day on Wednesday by going hiking with friend Cara Santana, 28, at Hollywood's Runyon Canyon.

The designer wore an all-black sports outfit of leggings and a black jumper with her hair tied into a messy bun. Cara wore a similar outfit, opting for a butterfly graphic print hooded sweat top teamed with black leggings and brown trainers.

Meanwhile, Nicole Richie's online series Candidly Nicole debuted a few months ago with a positive reception.

In a recent episode of her AOL show,  the 5ft 1in star shows how she clearly likes to get involved in all kinds of sport and exercise as she shoots some hoops with NBA star Lazar Hayward.
