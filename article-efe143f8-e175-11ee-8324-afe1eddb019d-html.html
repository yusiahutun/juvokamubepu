Thermo-Kool promotes Moss to VP, comptroller | Free News

Thermo-Kool recently announced the promotion of Paul Moss to vice president and comptroller.

“Paul has proven to be a reliable and trusted source regarding all aspects of finance that impact both the present and future operations of the company,” Thermo-Kool President and CEO Rand McLaughlin said. “I appreciate his honest and judicious input when it pertains to important and delicate business matters.”

Moss began at Thermo-Kool in 2015 as accounting manager, a role that is responsible for accounting, credit and payroll departments.

Thermo-Kool is located in Laurel and is a leading manufacturer of walk-in refrigeration and blast chillers/shock freezers.
