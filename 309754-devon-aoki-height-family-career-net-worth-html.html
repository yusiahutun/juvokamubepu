Devon Aoki height, family, career and net worth

Devon Edwenna Aoki is an American actress and fashion model. She is commonly known for playing the role of Suki in the 2003 action film 2 Fast 2 Furious. Aoki also portrayed Miho in the 2005 neo-noir crime anthology film Sin City. As a model, she graced the covers of many international fashion magazines such as Vogue and Harper's Bazaar. For many people in the fashion world, Devon Aoki is an icon who gave fashion enthusiasts something to strive for and look up to.

Early life

Although Devon Aoki was born in New York, she grew up in California. She went to school in the UK, where she joined The American School in London. The actress started modelling at the age of 13. She had the interest after her godmother introduced Kate Moss to her. Her dream of becoming a fashion model came true in 1997 when she made her runway debut.

While growing up, many people thought she was Asian. Although her looks suggest so, Devon Aoki's ethnicity is multiracial. Her father is Japanese, and her mother is of German and English descent. She can speak fluent English because she spent most of her young life in London and even attended school there.
