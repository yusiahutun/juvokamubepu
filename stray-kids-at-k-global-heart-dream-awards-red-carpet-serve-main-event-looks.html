Stray Kids at K Global Best Artist Award red carpet serves majestic looks in black and white co-ordi

Stray Kids are making the K-pop fandom swoon appearing in majestic outfits at the K Global Heart Dream Awards 2023. Later, the K-pop superstars also snagged multiple awards at the ceremony.

This year, K Global Heart Dream Awards saw many top music K-pop acts attending the ceremony. From Stray Kids, Ateez to ITZY, the ceremony was truly star-studded with multiple K-pop stars’ presence.

Stray Kids at K Global Heart Dream Awards red carpet serves majestic looks

Stray Kids attended the K Global Heart Dream Awards 2023 with all eight members.

Fans loved their coordinated black and white looks for the red carpet. While the group leader chose a white shirt and black pants for the occasion, ‘the dance machine’ of the group Hwang Hyunjin went all out wearing an all-black leather ensemble.

Felix was spotted sporting his casual blue hair look with a black and white suit and Lee Know and I.N chose a more formal look for the celebrity red carpet.

Stray Kids snag multiple awards as fans gush over the ‘main event’

Stray Kids were truly the main event at the K Global Heart Dream Awards as they snagged multiple trophies were the night. The fourth-generation K-pop trendsetters got K-Global Best Artist Award, the 4th Generation Hot Icon Male Group, and the ‘main prize’ aka Bonsang.

Gushing on the Stray Kids’ achievements, a Twitter fan swooned: “Main event for real! Winning the biggest award, performing last with 2 songs. Stray Kids congratulations!”

Another fan added: “Stray Kids the last performer, the main event!”

‘Best performers’

Stray Kids performed at the very end of the award show but fans agreed that the wait was well worth it. The Billboard 200 charter group performed two songs Item and SClass in an elaborate stage show truly setting a standard for the future generations of K-pop!
