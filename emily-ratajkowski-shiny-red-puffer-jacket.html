Emily Ratajkowski Braves the NYC Cold in a Shiny Red Puffer

Only Emily Ratajkowski can make 30-degree-weather dressing look cool.

Last week, the model stepped out in New York City in a sleek, cozy ensemble featuring the flashy puffer of our dreams and some trusty pants and sneakers.

Her fluffy jacket, the Willow Bria Short Puffer from Shoreditch Ski Club, comes in a glossy crimson and features an ample collar that almost doubles as a hood. EmRata wore it with black straight-leg trousers, a chunky black belt, black sneakers with neon orange soles, and her go-to black Loewe shoulder bag, which she's been wearing nonstop during fashion month.

She went for a casual evening glam: a bronzy smoky eye and a glossy brownish nude lip. She wore her straight hair down as she tried to keep warm in the chilly city.

BACKGRID

Ratajkowski, who walked several shows for New York Fashion Week earlier this month, has talked various times about her approach to dressing when she's off the runway, saying in an interview last year that comfy items, like flared leggings and sneakers, have become closet staples for her since she became a mother.

For her cover interview for Harper's BAZAAR last October, she wore a loose brown T-shirt layered over a pair of printed flared jersey leggings, and since then, she's shown off her relaxed style on several outings, including a date with Pete Davidson, a nature-filled getaway with toddler son Sylvester Apollo, and a courtside date with comedian Eric André.
