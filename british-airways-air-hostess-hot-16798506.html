British Airways air hostess leaves fans on cloud nine with stunning bikini snaps

Our Instagram Babe of the Week title goes to Jessica Parr — an air hostess for British Airways.

With over 27.1k followers, not to mention a job that allows her to explore the world, it's fair to say the blonde is living the high life.

Jessica's travel blog Instagram page — titled The Layover Life — is updated with one bikini-clad display after another, much to the delight of her loyal fans.

Sharing the spotlight, Parr also introduces her online audience to epic views of the places she travels.

From Tel Aviv to Thailand, the body confident starlet seems to rarely stay in one place for too long.

Like many online voices, Jessica's shares tidbits of her personal life online, as she recently announced her engagement news by posing with her sparkling ring.

Taking to her profile earlier this week, Parr updated fans from a luxury hotel in Palma, Spain.

Lapping up the sun rays poolside, the English rose donned a plunging red and white striped two-piece, which featured short, frilled sleeves.

Relaxing on a lounger, Jessica flashed a winning smile for the camera as she admitted she was "not even slightly ready" for her engagement holiday to end.

Responding to the post, a viewer marvelled: "Looking gorgeous, girl," alongside a heart-eyed emoji.

Another raved: "You're glowing, babe."

Adding to the commentary, an Instagram user quipped: "Engaged is a good look on you," followed by a fire symbol.

As well as wowing admirers with her swimwear displays, Jessica is also known to pose in her official British Airways uniform.

Capturing the iconic red, white and blue attire in a selfie last month, the model revealed she was looking forward to her upcoming trips.

The online sensation told her followers: "Back to work after the the absolute god-send that is my part-time week.

"Next three weeks of flying is jam-packed with some exciting trips. First stop — Denver."

Praising the air hostess' plane-ready appearance, a viewer remarked: "You look stunning, as always," alongside a heart-eyed emoji.

"British Airways uniforms look so lovely," another commented.
