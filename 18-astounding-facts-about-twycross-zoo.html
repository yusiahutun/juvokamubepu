Home to over 500 animals

Twycross Zoo is home to a diverse array of more than 500 animals, representing over 125 different species. From majestic lions to playful primates, you’ll have the opportunity to see a wide range of captivating creatures up close.

Conservation-focused zoo

Twycross Zoo is not just a place for entertainment; it is committed to conservation and the protection of endangered species. Through various initiatives and partnerships, the zoo actively contributes to global conservation efforts.

Internationally renowned primate collection

Twycross Zoo boasts one of the most extensive primate collections in the world. With over 60 different primate species, including orangutans, gorillas, and lemurs, it offers an unparalleled opportunity to learn about these incredible creatures.

Birthplace of the World Primate Centre

Twycross Zoo is proud to be the birthplace of the World Primate Centre, a leading research and education facility dedicated to primate conservation and welfare. Scientists and experts from around the globe collaborate here to advance our understanding of primates.

Educational programs and workshops

Twycross Zoo offers a range of educational programs and workshops, designed to inspire and educate visitors of all ages. From school visits to interactive sessions, these programs provide valuable insights into the animal kingdom and conservation efforts.

Close encounters with giraffes

Twycross Zoo offers a unique opportunity to get up close and personal with giraffes. Visitors can embark on the Giraffe Walk, where they can learn about these graceful creatures from experienced zookeepers and even hand-feed them.

The critically endangered Amur leopard

Twycross Zoo is one of the few zoos in the world that houses the critically endangered Amur leopard. Through breeding programs and conservation initiatives, the zoo plays a crucial role in preserving this majestic species.

Home to an extensive aviary

Twycross Zoo features an impressive aviary that is home to a multitude of beautiful bird species from around the world. Visitors can marvel at the vibrant colors and melodies of parrots, macaws, and a variety of other feathered friends.

Exciting daily animal talks

At Twycross Zoo, visitors have the opportunity to attend daily animal talks conducted by knowledgeable zookeepers. These talks provide fascinating insights into the behavior, habitats, and conservation efforts of various animal species.

Focus on animal welfare

Twycross Zoo places great emphasis on animal welfare, ensuring that all animals receive the highest standard of care. The zoo’s dedicated team of keepers and veterinarians work tirelessly to create a comfortable and enriching environment for every resident.

Pioneering primate research

The research conducted at Twycross Zoo has played a vital role in advancing our understanding of primates. The zoo has been at the forefront of numerous studies, leading to groundbreaking discoveries and contributing to primate conservation worldwide.

Award-winning gardens

In addition to its incredible collection of animals, Twycross Zoo is also renowned for its beautifully landscaped gardens. The zoo’s stunning horticultural displays have earned it several awards and make for a delightful setting while exploring the zoo.

Zoovolunteer program

Twycross Zoo offers a Zoovolunteer program, allowing individuals to gain hands-on experience and contribute to the daily operations and care of the animals. It’s a fantastic opportunity for animal lovers to make a meaningful impact and learn from experienced professionals.

Home to one of the largest chimpanzee colonies in the UK

Twycross Zoo is home to one of the largest chimpanzee colonies in the UK. Visitors can observe these highly intelligent and social creatures in their spacious and enriched habitats, gaining a deeper understanding of their behavior and conservation needs.

Unique well-being initiatives

Twycross Zoo prioritizes the physical and mental well-being of its animals. Through innovative initiatives like the “Ape Heart Project” and “Penguin Beach Club,” the zoo ensures that all residents have access to high-quality healthcare and enrichment activities.

A leading center for primate conservation

Twycross Zoo has established itself as a leading center for primate conservation. The zoo collaborates with international organizations to protect endangered primates in the wild, contributing to vital research, habitat restoration, and community engagement projects.

Interactive animal encounters

Visitors to Twycross Zoo can participate in interactive animal encounters, such as the Lemur Walk-through or Giant Tortoise Encounter. These experiences allow guests to get closer to the animals while learning about their unique characteristics and conservation status.

A fun-filled destination for the whole family

Twycross Zoo offers a full day of entertainment for the entire family. From exciting animal exhibits and educational activities to playgrounds and picnic areas, there’s something to keep everyone entertained and engaged.

Visit Twycross Zoo and discover firsthand why it is a world-class destination for animal lovers and conservation enthusiasts alike. With its impressive collection of wildlife, commitment to conservation, and educational initiatives, it is truly an astounding place to explore.

Conclusion

Twycross Zoo is an incredible destination that offers a unique and unforgettable experience for both animal enthusiasts and families alike. With its remarkable collection of species, commitment to conservation and research, and exciting range of attractions, it’s no wonder why Twycross Zoo stands out as one of the premier wildlife facilities in the UK.

From the fascinating world of primates to the opportunity to encounter some of the rarest animals on Earth, visitors to Twycross Zoo are guaranteed a day full of adventure, education, and enjoyment. FAQs

Q: Where is Twycross Zoo located?

A: Twycross Zoo is located in Atherstone, Warwickshire, in the heart of England.

Q: What are the opening hours of Twycross Zoo?

A: Twycross Zoo is open from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm every day of the week.

Q: How much does it cost to visit Twycross Zoo?

A: Ticket prices vary depending on the age of the visitor. It is advisable to check the official Twycross Zoo website for the most up-to-date pricing information.

Q: Can I bring my own food to Twycross Zoo?

A: Yes, visitors are allowed to bring their own food and have picnics in the designated outdoor areas of the zoo.

Q: Are there any discounts available for Twycross Zoo tickets?

A: Yes, Twycross Zoo offers discounts for students, seniors, and families. Additionally, purchasing tickets online in advance can often result in discounted rates.

Q: Can I interact with the animals at Twycross Zoo?

A: While direct interaction with most animals is not permitted, Twycross Zoo does offer various hands-on experiences and encounters with select species. These experiences can be booked in advance and are subject to availability.
