Gigi Hadid's cute daughter Khai seen in rare photos and she's 'growing so fast'

Gigi Hadid shared a rare glimpse into her life with her daughter Khai and fans can’t believe how big the model’s child is now. The model shares her two-year-old daughter with singer Zayn Malik.

The supermodel, 28, posted her “best” summer pictures featuring her toddler, and fans have gone wild over how big Khai looks now. As Gigi keeps her daughter away from the spotlight, the rare pictures have given an insight into the mother-daughter duo’s summer days and their favorite activities.

Gigi Hadid shares pictures with daughter Khai

Gigi is living her “best” summer with her daughter Khai right by her side. The model shared a string of adorable pictures as they enjoyed some activities like veggie picking and boat riding.

“Best of summer,” the 28-year-old celebrity captioned the carousel of images.

In one of the images, Gigi snapped a picture of Khai and showed her long locks while the toddler enjoyed a boat ride at a lake with ducks.

Fans go wild over ‘how big’ Khai is now

The pictures attracted many comments and fans totally lost it over how big Khai looks now. Gigi and Zayn welcomed their daughter on September 19, 2020, and she will soon turn three.

“Khai is growing so fast,” one fan commented. Another one said: “She has grown so much.”

“She’s so big already?” a third one questioned. “Her hair?”

“Can’t believe how big she is! Feels like she was born yesterday,” someone else noted.

Another fan added: “Your little girl is getting so big!”

Gigi was recently arrested

The cute pictures with Khai come following reports that Gigi was briefly arrested on her way to the Cayman Islands for marijuana possession.

As per reports at the time, airport officials found a “small amount” of marijuana during security checks and the model’s rep clarified that she “purchased the marijuana legally”.

The supermodel appeared to brush off the reports after her arrest and posted a collection of vacation snaps on Instagram alongside the caption: “All’s well that ends well.”

One of the images showed the supermodel and her friend next to beautiful turquoise water, while the other snaps featured some of the food they enjoyed during the vacation.
