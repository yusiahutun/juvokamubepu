Harry Styles Looks Just Like Mick Jagger on the Cover of Another Man Magazine

Another Man magazine tapped Harry Styles to front its newest issue, but we're pretty sure that's a young Mick Jagger on the cover. The 22-year-old One Direction member showed off his three covers and unreal likeness to the Rolling Stones frontman on Instagram today.

The shoots—conducted by Willy Vanderperre, Alasdair McLellan and Ryan McGinley—paint the singer in a bad-boy, vintage rock star image with leather chokers and tousled hair, but also show his soft side in a black and white knitted look. And if Styles wasn't already dipping his toes in legendary rock 'n' roll territory, he was also interviewed by Paul McCartney for the cover story (along with Chelsea Handler).

Styles' uncanny resemblance to the music legend helped land him a major movie role too. He's rumored to be playing Jagger in an upcoming biopic called Exile on Main Street: A Season in Hell with the Rolling Stones, which is set to follow the band's journey of writing the album that followed Sticky Fingers. It's based off the book of the same name, written by Robert Greenfield, and Andy Goddard is reportedly directing.
