Spooky Read for Halloween

Discover all things spooky in a new book by Chicago-based writer Selena Fragassi. Adapted from Ghosts of the Treasure Coast by history buffs and longtime locals Patrick and Patricia Mesmer, Fragassi’s The Ghostly Tales of the Treasure Coast takes readers through a series of haunted secrets and ghoulish fun pulled right from local history. Tales range from ghostly appearances and unexplained knocking at Sunrise Theatre to a family’s misfortunes at Fort Pierce’s Boston House (named after the city’s famed Boston Avenue). Pick up a copy at the Stuart Heritage Museum, Barnes & Noble, or online.
