What Is It? What Is It Worth?

I am hoping that you can provide me with details about my Coca-Cola Die-Cast Model Pedal Vehicle Silver Edition Plane. Does it have value?

P.S., EASLEY, S.C.

Your painted metal pedal car was fashioned from an earlier circa 1935 model called The Bomber. These planes are popular with collectors and sell for between $1,500 and $2,000. They usually measure about 48" long. The Coke logos are on both wings, making it even more collectible. These decals usually appear on the 1960s models, but this example may be even later.

Valued at: $1,500

*The estimates provided are preliminary only and subject to change based on firsthand inspection and further research. Appraisal prices refer to an item's fair market value, or what one might expect to pay for an object of similar age, size, color, and condition at auction.
