Salma Hayek dresses casually in a cardigan and jeans as she is seen leaving NYC

Actress Salma Hayek showed her fashion style as she was recently spotted at John F Kennedy International Airport in New York City.

The 56-year-old Mexican actress, who is also a naturalized US citizen, sported a striped cardigan over a white top, a pair of dark blue jeans and a pair black platform boots.

Hayek was seen carrying over her shoulder a large black Balenciaga hourglass bag that held all of her travel essentials. 

The actress wore her hair down and had it straightened.

She completed her travel attire with a pair of earring, a necklace featuring a large pendant on a silver chain, and a ring.

The ring appeared to be her wedding ring that she received when she wed French billionaire and Kerring CEO François-Henri Pinault on Valentine's Day in 2009. 

Hayek and Pinault have a 15-year-old daughter, Valentina Paloma Pinault, together. 

The actress is also a step-mother to Pinault's two children, son Francois and daughter Mathlide, from his first marriage to Dorothée Lepère and Pinault's son Augustin from his relationship with supermodel Linda Evangelista. 

In one of the photos of the sighting of Hayek at JFK airport, the actress was spotted looking at someone who was not seen in the picture. 

In that photo, the actress stood next to what appeared to be a help desk area after she got off the plane. 

Meanwhile, another image showed Hayek walking outside accompanied closely by a fit bald man who appeared to be her personal security guard.

The mysterious man photographed behind the actress was spotted wearing a black suit coat over a white long sleeved dress shirt, a pair of grey jeans, and a pair of back slip-on sneakers.

In the second and third photos of the sighting of Hayek at JFK Airport, the Oscar-nominated actress could also be seen holding what appeared to be a light-colored scarf or shawl.

The sighting of Hayek touching down on a flight at JFK Airport in New York City comes was not the only time that Hayek has displayed her incredible fashion sense.

In a June 14 appearance on Good Morning America to promote the Season 6 premiere of the British anthology series Black Mirror, the actress looked literally shredded as she rocked a blacked ribboned dress on the ABC morning television news show.

Hayek completed her all black ensemble with a black oversized blazer, a pair of black platforms, a pair of black sunglasses, a black bag, and a gold necklace.

The actress uploaded photos of her outfit and appearance on Good Morning America in both an Instagram post and on her Instagram stories.

'What a morning at @goodmorningamerica!,' Hayek captioned her June 14 Instagram post, which Good Morning America reposted on its own Instagram stories.

'Always so much fun! So excited for you all to watch my episode of Black Mirror ‘Joan is Awful’ with @annefrances out tomorrow on @netflix."
