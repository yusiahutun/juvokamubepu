All The Guest Stars On Evil, So Far

Many people may think their boss is the devil, but in "3 Stars," that hyperbole is one assistant's (Dascha Polanco) reality. 

Byron Duke, a famous theater producer, crosses the line from demanding to potentially demonic, so his assistant brings it to the attention of the Church. Kristen, David, and Ben are called in to check out whether narcissism or something far more sinister is to blame.

Ben is able to explain away some of the concerns, but in the end, there may be more at work that defies explanation.

Finding yourself a fan of the over-the-top Byron? John Glover also recurred on The Good Wife as Jared Andrews, and reprised the role for one episode of The Good Fight.
