    How to Choose the Best Claw Clips for Thin Hair?

    1. Check the Grip

    One of the most important factors to consider before buying the best claw clips for thin hair is the grip. Claw clips with tight grips may put excessive pressure on your scalp, leading to hair follicle damage. You may consider buying a claw clip with a gentle yet firm grip to hold your hair locks in place without tugging at them.

    2. Check the Size

    To get the best claw clips for thin hair, ensure it’s neither too small nor too big. Small claw clips can’t put stress on your locks, and bigger ones may not be able to hold your hair in place. Hence, you may get a claw clip 2-4.5 inches long to keep your hairstyles from looking unkempt and messy.
