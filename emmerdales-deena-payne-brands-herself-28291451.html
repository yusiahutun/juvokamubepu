Emmerdale's Deena Payne brands herself a 'tart' as she recalls slapping soap co-star

Emmerdale actress Deena Payne has recalled brutally slapping one of her co-stars while on set of the ITV soap.

Deena played Viv Hope from 1993 until 2011, wife to Bob and mum to Cathy and Heath. Famous for her mini-skirts and back-combed hair, Viv was involved in plenty of scandals throughout her tenure before she was brutally killed off in a fire.

Opening up in new book 50 Years of Emmerdale, actress Deena confessed to "hitting" one of her closest co-stars during a rather brutal scene.

Kelly Windsor, played by Adele Silva, was making her move on Viv's hubby Bob after her failed marriage to Roy Glover when Viv discovered her betrayal.

Speaking of the tense moment, Deena confessed: "I remember having a scene where I had to give her a right-hander, and she had to slap me back.

"And the director said, 'Hit her!' and I bloody did and she hit me back!"

Speaking of their relationship, the star elaborated: "It was a fractious relationship between Kelly and Viv. They were both quite vibrant personalities, and they clashed.

"Kelly was her father's daughter, and when he went, she started to go off the rails a bit. Viv was a tart with a heart and that rubbed off on Kelly – but she was more tart than heart!"

It comes after Deena teased her character Viv could still be alive in an exclusive chat with Daily Star.

She explained: "You never know, do you? They never saw my body - in fact, they didn’t even put me in a graveyard in Emmerdale, they just sent my gubbins back down south - I don’t even know where they went!

Deena added: "Terry's got a really long beard but no hair on the top and Viv has got quite long hair, and probably just as much hair on her chin as he has! And they would be a bit hippy-like. They would come back as real free hippies, that kind of thing."
