Every holiday season we wait patiently — or not too patiently — for Sephora to roll out its gift sets. Why, you ask? Well, not only are these kits perfect for gift-giving (they’re basically already wrapped), but they include some of the buzziest new and best-selling products at the retailer. Often, Sephora’s holiday gifts even have a major value compared to their current prices giving you a ton of freebies.

This year, TikTokers have been losing it over the newbies, like Olaplex’s Style & Strengthen Hair Set and Tatcha Dewy Skin Essentials Set. Of course, these all make great gifts. But if you’ve been wanting to try viral products, like Rare Beauty’s Liquid Blush, picking them up in a value set gets you the best bang for your buck. There are even stellar sex toys this year so it might be time to pick up a vibrator for you and one for a friend. And don’t miss the Sephora Collection sets, which have incredible value.
