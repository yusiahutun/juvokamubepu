Tailored to perfection | South China Morning Post

 

Ascot Chang has been at the pinnacle of exceptional men's tailoring for decades and, with clientele including Leonardo DiCaprio, Kirk Douglas, Andy Warhol, Tommy Hilfiger, Richard Nixon and George H. W. Bush among others, there is plenty to celebrate.

To celebrate its 60th anniversary, the luxury tailor shop is holding an exhibition at the Oval Atrium at ifc mall from September 5 to 9. It is also launching a series of limited-edition items, including a detachable collar set and a pocket square cardholder set.
