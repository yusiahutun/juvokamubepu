Sex dolls display 'human movement' after upgraded skeletons installed

Sex dolls with futuristic human-like skeletons have hit the market.

Stunning images show one doll being able to carry out full yoga moves thanks to the increased movement.

Sex doll supplier, Silicone Lovers, said the new designs were devised as part of a range of new upgrades.

Co-founder, Louie Love, told Daily Star Online: "The new release flexi skeletons are super flexible and allow for more natural, human-like posing for both modelling/photography and sexual positions.

"We’ve had what is known as an EVO skeleton for a while from brands like PIPER which enables dolls to squat but now skeletons are becoming even more evolved with double-jointed arms and legs so that they can squat, stretch and kneel like never before.
