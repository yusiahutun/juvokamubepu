Top 10 Goojara alternatives to watch movies, anime, and series in 2023

Therefore, it is better to consider Goojara alternatives if you do not want your streams interrupted.

Is Goojara legal?

Goojara is not a legal site. The website allows you to download movies and television shows sourced suspiciously. It is the legality of the site that makes it swap domains often. For example, goojara.ch no longer works. Instead, you will have to use goojara.to. However, you also have another option of using goojara.club.

Best Goojara alternatives for watching movies, anime, and series

If you have enjoyed Goojara.ch movies and television shows, you can still access them on other platforms that offer even better services. Here are ten Goojara alternatives you should consider in 2023.

1. CONtv

CONtv is the best platform for Comic-Con fans. If you do not want to watch movies on Goojara, you can head over to CONtv to get countless movies and television shows. In addition, you will access all the hilarious, weird, and terrifying content loved by comic fans.

CONtv helps you discover new movies and television shows. Furthermore, it curates genre content for passionate fans to share. You can access CONtv for free with ads or pay a fee of $6.99 to stream without ads.

2. Crackle

Crackle is one of the best sites similar to Goojara. It is one of the few legitimate applications on the internet that gives users access to free streaming television shows and movies. However, unlike Goojara, Crackle has many originals, giving it a competitive edge compared to other services.

Crackle supports ads where programmes get interrupted by commercials. However, it compensates by offering high-quality content in a library of over 1,000 films and TV series. Also, you can get Crackle on any device, including mobile phones, connected TVs, web browsers, and tablets.

3. Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is the number one online resource for anime. It offers a library of over 1,000 anime titles and 30,000 episodes. You can also catch your favourite shows and movies for free or for a small fee.

4. Funimation

Funimation has fronted itself as one of the best online resources for anime lovers. If you like the free movies on Goojara, you can access over 10,000 episodes and movies from the Funimation library of dubs and subs. Funimation offers free and premium content. The premium content costs $5.99 per month. However, the free titles come with ads, which is worth it considering its title offerings.

5. Pluto TV

Pluto TV is a better alternative to Goojara because of the number of TV channels it offers for free. The website gives you access to more than 100 channels. Also, Pluto TV is the home of free live TV because it has over 250 different live TV streams.

6. ShareTV

ShareTV offers a unique way of watching television shows online. The website allows you to watch episodes of some of your favourite television series. Furthermore, it has every show you can think of with character guides and a countdown to the next new episode.

ShareTV also has an online community of television show fans. The members share their ideas and the best titles to watch and show you where to stream. Understand that ShareTV only offers TV shows and not films. All titles are free.

7. The Roku Channel

The Roku Channel is perhaps the best platform for streaming movies, television shows, and anime. You will not get bored when using the service because it gives you access to more than 500,000 movies and TV episodes from thousands of free and paid channels.

8. Tubi

Tubi outshines many free streaming platforms or sites like Goojara because of its content library.
