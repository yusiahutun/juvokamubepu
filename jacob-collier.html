Jacob Collier is a British singer and musician who is gaining major attention after getting nominated at the 2021 Grammy Awards for 'Album of the Year.'

Jacob Collier's Wiki-Bio; Age, Birthday, Girlfriend, Family, Susan Collier 

Jacob Collier was born on August 1, 1994, and as of the writing of this article, is 26-years of age. He was primarily raised by his mother, Susan aka Suzie Collier, in North London alongside his two younger sisters. 

It is not wrong to say, Collier inherited music genes. His mother Susan, is a violinist, conductor, and professor at the Royal Academy of Music's Junior Academy. Similarly, his maternal grandfather Derek Collier was a violinist.

Jacob Collier is a Grammy Award Winning Musician; What is his Net Worth?

Jacob, who is a multi-instrumentalist, started uploading homemade content to YouTube in 2011. Mostly there were rearrangements of the classic songs. Soon his activity was noticed by music giants such as Quincy Jones, and as they say, the rest is history.

In less than seven years since signing in the management company of Quincy Jones, the British singer has released four studio albums and eight singles and has won two Grammy awards. In addition to that, he has helped score for big-budgeted movies such as The Boss Baby and collaborated with tech giants such as Samsung.
