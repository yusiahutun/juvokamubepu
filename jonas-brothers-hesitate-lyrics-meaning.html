Jonas Brothers Heistate Song Lyrics Meaning Explained

If the idea of a new Jonas Brothers album feels out-of-this-world to you, then you're not alone. As eldest sibling Kevin Jonas told BAZAAR.com, "It’s surreal, being able to do this again."

But, believe it or not, the trio is back and better than ever, now with a brand-new LP, Happiness Begins, under their belt. The brothers get especially mushy on the album, which is laden with romantic tracks, including "Hesitate," which arrives towards the end of the track list.

"This is an important song," Joe Jonas told Apple Music, dedicating it to his now-wife Sophie Turner. "I wrote it for my significant other, Sophie, and it's one of those love letters that you write to your partner saying, 'I'll be there no matter what.'"

Joe and Sophie recently became husband and wife with a surprise ceremony in Las Vegas, but are expected to tie the knot with a bigger wedding this summer. "We had to do a legal marriage before we did a real big one," Joe told BAZAAR.com.
