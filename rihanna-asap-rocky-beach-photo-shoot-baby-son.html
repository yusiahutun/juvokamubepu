Rihanna and A$AP Rocky Coordinate Looks on Beach Photo Shoot with Their Baby Son

Rihanna and boyfriend A$AP Rocky are showing off their baby boy to the world for the first time.

The singer and the rapper embarked on a beach photo shoot over the weekend with their little one. The new parents matched in black looks and silver jewelry, while their son was wrapped up in a cozy red, white, and beige blanket.

Rihanna looked stunning in a breezy black halter-neck dress by Chanel with a monogram print and a silver embellishment at the neck. She finished the sophisticated ensemble with a silver cuff bracelet, matching drop earrings, and a large silver ring. For glam, the "Love on the Brain" singer wore smoky eye makeup, a bronzy lip, and a messy beach hairdo.

Rocky, meanwhile, wore a full leather look, consisting of a sleeveless top, cargo pants, and chunky boots. He, too, accessorized with a silver bracelet and earrings.

The musicians were seen smiling while walking by the ocean with their son, whose name they have not yet revealed. Their beach photo shoot came hours after the pair shared the first look at their baby boy, a TikTok video featuring the happy baby sitting in his car seat.

Rihanna and Rocky—who were friends for years before making their relationship official in 2020—welcomed their child on May 13, per TMZ.

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight last month, the multi-hyphenate opened up about embracing motherhood and revealed her favorite part about spending time with her baby.

"Oh my god, the mornings, like, seeing his morning face!" she told the outlet on the red carpet for the premiere of her Savage x Fenty Show Vol. 4. "Seeing a baby with, like, little bags and waking up and they're just, like, startled. They're trying to figure out where they're at. It's the cutest, it's my favorite part of the day."
