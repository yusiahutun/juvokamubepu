Bella Hadid Pays Tribute to Boyfriend Adan Banuelos on His Birthday

Happy birthday to rodeo star—and boyfriend to Bella Hadid—Adan Banuelos! The supermodel kicked off the Mexican-American cowboy’s birthday with a series of sweet candid photographs shared to her Instagram Story on Monday night.

Leading the pack of photos was a pastoral snap of Hadid and Banuelos embarking on an evening horseback ride. Their horses standing close together, the two shared a kiss, and Banuelos leaned over to put his arm around Hadid’s waist while the model wrapped hers around his neck. “Happy birthday my love,” she captioned the photo. “I love life with you,” she also wrote over a since-deleted snap.

Other snaps show a rare glimpse into the couple’s day-to-day life in Fort Worth, Texas, where Hadid reportedly recently moved to be closer to Banuelos. These include pictures of the couple enjoying a fireworks show, kissing inside a rodeo stadium, and matching in cowboy hats.

Hadid and Banuelos were first romantically linked in October 2023, after TMZ published paparazzi photos of the two kissing and embracing. Since then, Hadid—a lifelong equestrian—has embraced the western life, even participating in rodeo competitions herself. In January, she competed in an event hosted by the National Cutting Horse Association at Bosque Ranch in Weatherford, Texas, ultimately landing eighth place. And just last week, Hadid shared a video of herself participating in yet another riding contest, revealing in the caption that she and her stallion, Tucker, had “made it to the second go of our first Non Pro aged event together.”

“My devotion stemmed from my mom’s love of horses. I have been riding since I could walk and the fact that my mom knew everything about horses really helped my passion grow,” the model told Porter in 2015. “You know how every model is like, ‘I do yoga.’ Well, I find horses have the same effect. You have to put your ego aside and concentrate on making the horse do the things you want it to do, and move in the way you want it to move.”
