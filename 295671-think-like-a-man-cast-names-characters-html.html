Think Like a Man cast names and characters

Typically, when we begin watching any American comedy film, there are certain things we look for in the title sequence. The opening credits, for one, give us details of who features in the film. Once we see certain names in the opening credits, then we are assured of the fact that the film will be a pretty funny one. Such names include Kevin Hart, Michael Ealy, Meagan Good, Regina Hall, Terrence Jenkins, and Taraji P. Henson, to mention a few. The names mentioned above, among others, make up the Think Like a Man cast. Together with other amazing actors skilled in their field, this ensemble manages to keep us glued to our screens in the film adaptation of Steve Harvey’s book: Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man. Their flawless acting is what fueled the production of the movie’s sequel, Think Like a Man Too, and it is also what has us wishing that there will be a Think Like a Man 3.

Honestly, who can argue with the fact that Regina Hall and Kevin Hart make the perfect duo in a movie? And who dare refute the fact that Michael Ealy is an irresistible actor who keeps women glued to their screens? These unparalleled actors, among others listed below, form the principal dramatis personae of Think Like a Man (2012), an American romantic comedy film based on Steve Harvey’s 2009 book: Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man. The movie, which runs for about two hours and 2 minutes, revolves around the following four storylines of the couples involved:

  • ‘The Mama’s Boy’ vs ‘The Single Mom.’
  • ‘The Non-Committer’ vs ‘The Girl Who Wants the Ring.’
  • ‘The Dreamer’ vs ‘The Woman Who Is Her Own Man.’
  • ‘The Player’ vs ‘The 90 Day Rule Girl.’

The Think Like a Man storyline follows the lives of a group of women who read Steve Harvey’s book and use the writer’s advice to get their men in line. Their partners find out about the women’s plot to use Steve Harvey’s Think Like a Man book against them, and they set out to turn the tables on the women. Will the men, who are utterly angered by the fact that their very own (Steve Harvey) could go against them, manage to outshine the ladies? Only watching Think Like a Man full movie will tell.

    The IMDb rating for Think Like a Man is 6.6/10, while Rotten Tomatoes’ Tomatometer rating for the movie is 54% with an average rating of 5.5/10 and an audience score of 75%. However, if you ask me, I think that Think Like a Man is a movie worth higher ratings, but that is just but my opinion. The movie uses wit and humour to bring out the best of it characters in the most natural way possible. With the wonderful reception it got from its audience, the movie gave birth to a sequel: Think Like a Man Too, or if you like, Think Like a Man 2.

    Think Like a Man 2 cast is pretty much the same as the one listed above, albeit it has been tweaked a little bit here and there. All in all, the sequel does not disappoint. The sequel follows the couples who are now in Las Vegas, attending a friend’s wedding. The men separate from the women, and the two groups both seek to have fun in their own ways. However, both the guys and the ladies seem to get carried away with all the fun and land themselves in serious trouble. All this happens in the movie, while still giving the characters a chance to battle with their problems such as intimacy, infidelity, and career-related issues. It is definitely worth a watch.

    The Think Like a Man cast is what endears us to the film. Without a doubt, they perfectly fit the roles they play, and they work nicely to give us the Think Like a Man ending we all pray for while watching the movie.
