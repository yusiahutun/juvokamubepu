Christi Zook Lukasiak Bio, Parents, Net Worth

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: June 04, 2019

Christi Zook Lukasiak is an American TV reality star. She is mostly known for the show, Dance Moms. Although the reality show brought Christi to the spotlight, she has always been a hard working woman. She previously owned a children's fitness center named Stretch-N-Grow. Following her success in TV, she later sold the business.

Find out how a mother hit it big time in the showbiz along with information on her bio, family, net worth, and more!

Christi Lukasiak Bio-LIke-Wiki

Christi was born on the 3rd of March 1977 in Churchill, Pennsylvania. However, she was raised by her maternal grandparents in Naval housing in Groton, Connecticut. 

Hailing from the US, Christi is an American by nationality and possesses white ethnicity.

As for her measurements, the beautiful blonde stands tall with a height of 5 feet 10 inches (1.78 m). She has managed to keep herself fit despite having her handsful with her professional life.

Christi is allergic to synthetic perfumes. Christi and her daughters: Chloe & Clara Lukasiak can only wear natural perfumes.

Married Life: Husband & Kids

With regards to Christi's personal life, she is a happily married woman. Christi has been with Marc Lukasiak for more than two decades now.

The couple had their engagement on the day of Christmas Eve in 1998. The duo got married a year later in 1999. 

Christi with husband Marc and daughters Chloe (second from left) & Clara Lukasiak (second from right) at The Ritz-Carlton, Grand Cayman in May 2019 (Photo: Christi Lukasiak's Instagram)

Christi and Marc welcomed their first child, a daughter called Chloe Elizabeth on 26 May 2001. They had their second daughter named Clara Alexandra on the 17th August 2009. 

Reality Show And Fame

The reality TV star has a very active family. 

Her husband, Marc Lukasiak, is a Director of marketing and communications at the Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh. Her two daughters are both internet personalities in their own right. 

Focusing on Christi, she rose to fame with the reality TV Show, Dance Moms. The show hosted a dance competition with moms and their daughters. Christi was with the show from the start of the first season, aired on 13th July 2011, to an episode centered on her and Chloe on 31st of October 2017.

The show undoubtedly put the family on the spotlight of Hollywood. During her time with the show, she also made guest appearances on Anderson Live in 2012, Celebrity Page in 2017, and many more. 

Out of her ride on the wave of fame of the reality show, Christi - along with her two daughters - has done incredibly well for herself. Christi has over 2 million followers on Instagram, while Chloe and Clara each have 6.1 million and 1 million fans on the social media platform.
