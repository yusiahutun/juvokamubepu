Meet Baker Jonny Manganello! His Bio, Age, Wife, Job, Height, Is It Cake?

Jonny Manganello is a TikTok star whose videos for baking lookalike cakes were loved by many. Especially, his expertise in creating fast-food cakes like McDonald’s and Taco Bell put him right amongst the top bakers in the field. No wonder, he was one of the contestants on Netflix’s 2022 reality baking competition “Is It Cake?” 

This “Jonny Manganello Bio” explores his life. 

Jonny Manganello On Netflix’s Is It Cake?

Netflix’s “Is It Cake?” premiered on March 18, 2022, and it saw nine bakers including Jonny, going head to head to create hyper-realistic cakes to impress the panel of judges — Bobby Moynihan, Loni Love, Michael Yo, Fortune Feimster, Rebecca Black, King Princess, Arturo Castro, and Heidi Gardner.

As for the co-contestants joining Jonny in this inaugural season, they were — Andrew Fuller, April Julian, Dessiree Salaverria, Hemu Basu, Justin Rasmussen-Ellen, Nina Charles, Sam Cade, and Steve Weiss — each blessed with their own set of skills.

However, since this was a reality competition, there could be only one winner.

Spoilers Alert: Sadly, it wasn’t Jonny.

Yes, after a tight battle between the final three, it was Andrew Fuller who beat out fellow finalists, Hemu Basu and April Julian. The final task for the top 3 was to fool the three celebrity guests, where Andrew created an intricately detailed leather-looking suitcase.

As promised, Andrew walked out with the £50,000 grand prize plus the extra £30,000 he won over the “winner stays on” relay part of the competition.

But he wasn’t the only one who went home with cash in his hand. Hemu was awarded $10,000 for her work in the “winner stays on,” meanwhile, April got $15,000.

Also, our star, Jonny earned himself $10,000 (initially $5,000, which was doubled in the “Cake Or Cash” round).

Before the show’s premiere, Jonny had made an IG post captioned, “Omg NETFLIX here I come! So excited to be a part of Is It Cake? Can’t say much, but this is a special one.”

And it indeed was a “special one” for him.

Jonny Manganello Job

Jonny Manganello is a baker and a cake artist. He owned Jonny Cakes, a bakery that specialized in custom, super personalized cakes based in West Hollywood, California.

But unlike his “Is It Cake?” co-contestants, Jonny doesn’t have much experience in the baking field. According to Jonny, he only started baking in the summer of 2018. However, despite being relatively new, this talented baker made up for it by spending a sheer amount of time in the kitchen. “I do it every single day, all the time, and built a business for myself,” he explained.

Talking about the challenges he’s faced in his career, Jonny shared that he had trouble with pricing his cakes. Since it took him over two days to make one, a lot of people would be surprised by the amount of time, energy, and supplies, and of course the money. “If I’m not paying myself fairly, I’m just screwing myself over,” he remarked.

Besides running his shop, this Grosse Pointe South graduate also actively participated in any baking competitions (on or off-screen). Before “Is It Cake?”, Jonny was in Food Network’s Holiday Wars season 2. This was his first reality show, and he shared that it was a “dream come true.”

For any aspiring baker out there, Jonny asks them to  “work slow and steady.” He says that cake baking and decorating is artful as much as it is scientific. “Let your cakes cool completely before decorating, spread the work out over a few days, and take time away from your work so you can see it with a new set of eyes. Baking and decorating should be fun and calming. Don’t rush the process,” he explained.

Back in 2017, Jonny was still searching for love on dating apps.
