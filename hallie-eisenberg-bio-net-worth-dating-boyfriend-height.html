Who is Hallie Eisenberg? Bio and Wiki

Hallie Eisenberg was born on 2nd August 1992 as Hallie Kate Eisenberg. Further, she was born to American parents Betty Eisenberg (father) and Amy Eisenberg (mother) in East Brunswick, New Jersey, the USA. Her mother was a professional clown who worked at children’s birthday party in their hometown of New Brunswick. Whereas, her father ran a hospital and later became a college professor. She has two siblings; Kerry and Jesse Eisenberg.

Eisenberg holds American citizenship and belongs to Ashkenazi Jewish ethnicity. As per her bio, her zodiac sign is Leo. When it comes to her education, she attended American University in Washington, DC.
