Richard Marx is a Grammy-winning singer-songwriter.

With a career spanning over four decades, Richard Marx has achieved great success in the music industry, winning a Grammy Award for his song “Dance with My Father” in 2004.

Richard Marx, a true Chicagoan, was born and raised in the Windy City, where his passion for music began to take shape.
