14 Best Knee-High Boots for Fall

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Best Everyday Knee-High Boot

Khaite Derby Leather Knee-High Boots

Best Everyday Knee-High Boot

Khaite Derby Leather Knee-High Boots

Every well-dressed woman will get her money's worth when investing in Khaite's signature black leather Derby Boots. The equestrian-inspired boot has a roomy rounded toe with the smallest of heels that'll make running around in them a breeze, even on the busiest of days.

Best Statement Knee-High Boot

Schutz Maryana Pointed Toe Boot

Best Statement Knee-High Boot

Schutz Maryana Pointed Toe Boot

Credit: Nordstrom

Deemed one of the most comfortable heeled boots on the market, this embossed leather Schutz pair is an easy choice for those who want a functional style they can dress up or down. The 3.5-inch heel makes them ideal for every occasion, from long days in the office to nights spent out dancing.

Best Block-Heel Knee-High Boot

Dear Frances Elle Knee Boot

Best Block-Heel Knee-High Boot

Dear Frances Elle Knee Boot

Every Dear Frances shoe description features this quote by designer Jane Frances: "I look to develop investment styles with a quiet aesthetic; pieces that can be worn and cherished forever is the heart of our slow fashion ethos." And what's more timeless than this brown espresso suede knee-high block-heel boot? Nothing, really.

Most On-Trend Knee-High Boot

Miista Gray Finola Tall Boots

Most On-Trend Knee-High Boot

Miista Gray Finola Tall Boots

Knee-high boots look best when worn in, but this block heel boot by Miista comes pre-worn. There's gray smudging throughout the crinkled polished leather, which is perfectly on trend with the intentional wrinkling seen on the fall 2023 runways.

Best Riding Knee-High Boot

M.Gemi The Giulietta

Best Riding Knee-High Boot

M.Gemi The Giulietta

Now 63% Off

While riding boots were originally made for riding horses, these are for all the non-horse girls and horse girls out there. With on-trend lug soles, these updated riding boots are made for walking everywhere.

Most Unique Knee-High Boot

Hanifa Zoe Boot

Most Unique Knee-High Boot

Hanifa Zoe Boot

Credit: Hanifa

Black-owned brand Hanifa recently expanded its product offering to include its first-ever knee-high boots, and they're as chic as one would expect. They're made with sheep leather wrapped straps that run across the fronts of the boots—perfect for those who prefer to make their own trends.

Best '90s-Inspired Knee-High Boot

Reformation Nylah Nappa Knee Boot

Best '90s-Inspired Knee-High Boot

Reformation Nylah Nappa Knee Boot

Credit: Reformation

Stock up on your favorite Ref jeans and a pair of reliably chic boots in one fell swoop. Evidently, the '90s must have called up the Ref HQ and offered a couple suggestions for how to make the Nylah look like a true blast from the past. It has everything the best boots from the era have: a wearable block heel, a sexy square toe, and a sleek fit.

Best Metallic Knee-High Boot

Dries Van Noten Silver Cracked Leather Tall Boots

Best Metallic Knee-High Boot

Dries Van Noten Silver Cracked Leather Tall Boots

If more traditional Western boots aren't your style, Dries Van Noten's metallic grained leather version is a great alternative. Plus, the silhouette has been around for years, transcending seasons and trends with ease. The four-inch heel is also begging for a nonstop weekend out.

Best Western Knee-High Boot

Paris Texas Rosario Boots

Best Western Knee-High Boot

Paris Texas Rosario Boots

However, for those who want to lean into the cowboy boot trend, this Rosario Boot is a more traditional take with fashion-forward details. It's truly the best of both worlds with a bold red hue, croc-embossed calfskin, Western-inspired stitching, and a stacked Cuban heel.

Best Retro Knee-High Boot

Nomasei Twist Stretch Boots

Best Retro Knee-High Boot

Nomasei Twist Stretch Boots

Credit: Nomasei

A mod look if we've ever seen one, this '60s-inspired design is practically made for doing the twist. And it's pretty easy, too, with a two-inch heel built for dancing. Pair this go-go style with everything from pleated skirts to sweater dresses to complete the retro look.

Best Patent-Leather Knee-High Boot

Miu Miu Patent-Leather Block Heel Boots

Best Patent-Leather Knee-High Boot

Miu Miu Patent-Leather Block Heel Boots

The contrasting white toe is a refreshing detail that sets this vintage-inspired style apart from every other patent-leather boot on the market. And it's a less trendy Miu Miu footwear option if you're tempted to buy the satin ballet flats that are everywhere.

Best Logo Knee-High Boot

Gucci Women's GG Knee-High Boot

Best Logo Knee-High Boot

Gucci Women's GG Knee-High Boot
