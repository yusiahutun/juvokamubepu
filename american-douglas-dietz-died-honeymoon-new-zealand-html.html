American man Douglas Dietz died on his honeymoon in New Zealand

Friends have paid tribute to an American tourist who tragically died on his honeymoon in New Zealand last week after he left his new wife to go 'star-gazing' and crashed into a ditch.

Douglas Dietz, 29, and his bride Kathleen, both from the US state of Virginia, arrived in New Zealand on Saturday September 24.

The following Wednesday the newlyweds checked into a hotel in Martinborough, north of Wellington. Mr Dietz drove off alone sometime between 8pm and 9pm that night.

Ms Dietz (pictured at her wedding) became concerned and reported her husband missing when he had not returned by 3am

American tourist Douglas Dietz tragically died on his honeymoon in New Zealand last week after he crashed his rental car (pictured) into a ditch

His body was found in a Toyota Estima rental van at about 7am the next day after he crashed on a nearby rural road, Stuff reported.

Police confirmed he was not wearing a seatbelt.

Mr Dietz told his wife he left because he 'wanted to see the stars.'
