Who is 'Sister Wives' star Robyn Brown?

Robyn Brown was born on October 9, 1978. She was previously married to David Jessop. She had three kids with David - Dayton, Aurora, and Breanna Rose.

Later, Robyn met Kody in 2009 and got spiritually married in 2010. She was Kody's fourth wife but now has become the only wife.

Robyn and Kody legally married in 2014.

Who is 'Sister Wives' patriarch Kody Brown?

Kody Brown is the 54-year-old TLC's 'Sister Wives' patriarch. He was born into a polygamist family and followed the same lifestyle in his life.

He first met Meri Brown and got married in 1990. He then spiritually married Janelle in 1993 and Christine in 1994. 

Later, Kody divorced Meri in 2014 in order to legally marry his fourth wife Robyn. But, as of now, none of his wives except Robyn is involved with him in the polyamorous marriage.

'Sister Wives' Season 18 will release on August 20 at 10 pm ET on TLC.
