Minimalism, Shorter Beards, &amp; Natural Looks

In the ever-evolving landscape of men’s grooming, the beard continues to reign as a sexy symbol of style and individuality. As we head into 2024, the realm of facial hair takes on new dimensions, with a fusion of classic charm and contemporary trends reshaping the aesthetics of men’s beards. Post pandemic lockdowns, men’s beard trends for 2024 are a bit more tame but not at all boring.

After guys let their beards and hair grow while at home, now they’re getting creative with sleeker styles out and about in the world. 2024 is all about versatility with sophistication in beard styling. The prevailing trend revolves around a mix of length and neatness, with meticulously groomed beards stealing the spotlight. The emphasis is on tailored precision, where well-defined lines and shapes complement various face structures, enhancing features, and boosting confidence.

So, take inspo from the following hot people to inspire your 2024 look.

  • Jake Gyllenhaal

    Jake Gyllenhaal fits right in with 2024 trends according to Taylor Hooker, Marketing Manager and Grooming Expert at Jack Black. “Much like the hair trends, we’re going to see an all or nothing approach to facial hair,” she explains. “For our bearded guy, it’ll be less about sharp lines and structured beards, and more about an organic approach. He’s making what he’s got look good without a ton of muss or fuss (and investing in a quality beard oil for a soft, manageable beard).”

    Hooker sees a lot of clean shaven men, too. “I honestly think an all-or-nothing sentiment is going to be prevalent across both the hair and facial hair categories in 2024,” she says.

  • Donald Glover

    Donald Glover is another perfect example of this natural-looking but sleek and groomed beard style. It shows his sense of style while still being put together for the red carpet. But really, does he ever look bad? We’re going to say no.

  • Henry Cavill

    The always handsome Henry Cavill knows how to wear a beard that’s groomed without being too sleek. His neck is clean and his cheeks have nice lines—always.

  • Ryan Gosling

    We love when Ryan Gosling grows out his facial hair just enough. It’s shorter than other stars but no less impactful.

  • Andrew Garfield

    Andrew Garfield rocks a trendy laid-back beard that’s still groomed to perfection. Tina Sapia, Booksy Ambassador and owner of Sapia’s Barbershop in Clearwater, FL, sees beards getting shorter in 2024. “When it comes to grooming, beards may be getting shorter, and people are going into hairstyles that require less product, or ‘touchable styles’ as I like to call them,” she says. “Going into 2024, we’re noticing more people aim for a more natural look.”

  • Glen Powell

    Glen Powell has that natural look that’s still groomed for the red carpet. His longer hair really adds to the movie star look.

  • John Legend

    Who has a sharper beard than John Legend? No one. We’re sure he’s using his own brand, LOVED01, to keep it soft and hydrated.

  • Penn Badgley

    Penn Badgley let his beard and hair grow out during Covid 19 lockdowns. And he looked good. But we can’t help but love this groomed look on him with just enough beard to keep his mysterious vibe.

  • Michael B. Jordan

    Michael B. Jordan goes back and forth with a sleek beard or clean-shaved look. We can’t decide which is our favorite but we do love this in-between style.

  • Jason Momoa

    Jason Momoa has a the natural bearded look down maybe better than anyone. All he needs is a little grooming and he’s ready to hit the red carpet. We can’t image him without his iconic facial hair.

  • Drake

    Do you think Drake and Jayson Tatum are talking about who has the better beard? It’s a tie to us because they’re both groomed to perfection.

  • David Beckham

    David Beckham is going for that shorter bearded style that’s so popular, though it’s hard to tell with all his neck tattoos. (We’re not complaining!)

  • Zac Efron

    Zac Efron also grew out his beard while he wasn’t working but now that he has a big movie coming out and a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, he’s much more clean shaven.

  • Idris Elba

    Idris Elba‘s salt-and-pepper beard just adds to his sex appeal. Longer or shorter, he’s always perfectly groomed.

  • Joe Manganiello

    We love his full beard, but when Joe Manganiello has just this little bit of stubble, he’s especially handsome on the red carpet.
