JoJo Siwa fans 'love seeing her happy' again after 'devastating' grief

JoJo Siwa fans are glad to see their fave Dance Moms star happy again as she’s all smiles during her time away on a cruise, after tragically losing her dog earlier this year.

The 20-year-old lost her pooch Tooie (O2) to a fatal accident back in April, after only having him for two months. In a TikTok at the time, the star wrote: “My heart hurts so much to say that this morning he was involved in an accident and is now in puppy heaven.” JoJo was visibly ‘devastated’ by the loss of her pooch who she was ‘so close’ to.

JoJo Siwa has ‘the best 14 days’ on family cruise

JoJo Siwa regularly updates her followers with her happenings over on Instagram and TikTok, however, she seems to have been a little quiet on the front lately.

However, she recently updated her fans on ‘the best 14 days’ spent with her friends and family on a cruise, in Alaska, and fans are loving seeing her happy again.

In the caption of the post, the star wrote: “the BEST 14 days,” as she spent time on a cruise with both her family and friends.

JoJo can be seen spending quality time with her father, as well as breaking out into epic dances with friends as they enjoy the scenic views of the cruise.

Earlier this year, the star had an ’emotional’ family reunion after heading off to New Zealand and not being able to ‘see anyone’ for weeks while taking part in an undisclosed project.

Many other celebrity faces headed off to the same location around the same time, with some fans thinking a TV show may be in the works.

Siwa can be seen smiling throughout the clips, and at one point even looks overcome with emotion as she breaks down in (happy) tears.

Fans were also asking the star when she’d be back on Snapchat to which she replied: “Will be back tomorrow.” So more content from the influencer is underway!

Fans love seeing the star happy again

After JoJo’s grief earlier this year, fans in the comments are loving seeing how happy the star is surrounded by her family and friends.

“Her relationship with her dad is so wholesome,” penned one.

“Looks like you had an amazing trip!!! Love seeing you smile like that!.” exclaimed another.

Another wrote: “I’m glad you had fun! you deserve it.”

“Good to see you having fun and happy,” wrote another follower.

Following on with the theme, another said: “You deserve to have all the time off you need!!! Good to see you happy!”

JoJo Siwa’s home was broken into earlier this year

As well as the ‘devastating’ loss of her dog, JoJo Siwa also experienced a burglary in her house in May, whilst away from home.

Taking to her Instagram at the time, she said: “We were robbed last night at 2:40(ish) am, It was an armed robbery which is very scary.

This makes me sick to watch. “I’m just happy that my family and pups are safe. The most important thing to me. Do me a favor and hug your people extra tight tonight,” she continued.

“Life is SCARY. And you never know when anything could happen.”
