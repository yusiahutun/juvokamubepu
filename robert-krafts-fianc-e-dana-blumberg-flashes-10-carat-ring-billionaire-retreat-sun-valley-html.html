Robert Kraft's fiance Dana Blumberg flashes her 10-carat ring at billionaire retreat in Sun Valley

Dana Blumberg was seen flashing her giant 10-carat engagement ring while going on a stroll with her billionaire fiancé Robert Kraft on Day Three of Sun Valley.

The power couple joined the likes of Twitter CEO Parag Agrawal, Bill Gates and Disney's Bob Chapek at the 'billionaire summer camp.'  

Kraft CEO Robert, 81, who is worth an estimated $8.3 billion, popped the big question to the 47-year-old New York-based ophthalmologist Blumberg in March after dating for three years. 

He was spotted wearing a blue puff jacket, dark-colored pants and Nike sneakers at the resort town in Idaho, while Blumberg looked effortlessly in a white top, pink pants and brown open-heel sandals.

The news of the couple's engagement had been kept under wraps but Kraft's designer pal Tommy Hilfiger, 70, let it slip at the inaugural amfAR Gala Palm Beach event on March 5. 

The couple's wedding will be the second trip down the aisle for Kraft, who was married to his college sweetheart Myra from 1963 until her death from cancer in 2011.
