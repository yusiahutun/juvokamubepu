Really Kate? Gosselin loses her cool in front of her children during vicious fight over PIZZA of all things

It's not the first time, but it's been a while since we've seen Kate Gosselin completely lose her cool in front of the cameras.

Without ex-husband Jon Gosselin to push around, Kate lashes out at her best friend Jamie, and her nanny Ashley.

It all happens during a road trip, in a new episode Kate Plus 8, and the single mum doesn't mince her words.

Not happy: Kate Gosselin looses her temper on the latest episode of the TLC series Kate Plus 8

Fighting back: Kate's nanny and her friend Jamie refuse to put up with the single mum

The fight seems to stem from food rations and what the group is supposed to be eating each day.

Then things boil over when someone eats a piece of pizza that Kate insists was reserved for her  bodyguard Steve.

But the nanny, who is sitting inside of a motor home talking to Kate through an open window, gets in the last word when she threatens to quit saying: 'I’m more than happy to leave right now.' 

Peacemaker: Kate's bodyguard Steve tries to intervene in the vicious food fight

Distraught: As the fight rages on, Kate's children are clearly bothered by the drama and begin to cry

'Pizza is not a friggin’ big deal,' the nanny screams at Kate adding, 'I am sick of your dramatics.'

At one point, the fast food fight got so heated that it left Kate's children in tears, as the women bickered and exchanged insults.

Jamie playfully taunted Kate that she and the kids have all the pizza and french fries, which sent Kate into a rage, as she brought up the fact that at the rate that the food is being consumed there will be none left for Steve

'Steve wants a piece of pizza right now,' Kate yells, 'He reserved it last night.

Here we go: Kate puts her hands on her hips as she flies into one of her famous rages

Fun: Kate's children were much happier when they got a chance to go rafting during the road trip

'Hand it over. You guys can eat salads. Give him his pizza. That was rude,' she says.

Kate’s friends relent and hand the last slice to Maddy to hand to Steve. And that’s when things get out of hand.

Kate, a germaphobe, is livid that one of the women gave an unwrapped piece of pizza to a child with dirty hands to pass to Steve.

The family is on a field trip to Grand Canyon National Park.

TLC cancelled the show last month and this is its final season.

The new episode airs on Monday, September 5 on the TLC cable network.
