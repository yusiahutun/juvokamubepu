The late David Bowie was a man of many personae, a rock star defined by his experimentation with identity. Bowie’s shape-shifting musical genius was often captured by photographers like Terry O’Neill and personal shooter Mick Rock, two greats of celebrity portraiture, and countless others as it evolved over the decades. Take a look back at the life of the man who fell to Earth in these career-spanning photographs.
