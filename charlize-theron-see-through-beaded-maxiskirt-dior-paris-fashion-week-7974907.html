Charlize Theron Paired a Totally See-Through Beaded Maxiskirt With Black Underwear and a Button-Down

While plenty of stars are still currently arriving at Dior’s Spring Summer 2024 Paris Fashion Week show (hi, Jenna Ortega and JLaw!), Charlize Theron has already proven that no one’s doing it quite like she is — as if we needed the reminder.

On Tuesday, the actress arrived at the star-studded show’s photocall wearing the sartorial embodiment of “business on top, party on bottom.” For the occasion, Theron slipped into a plain white button-down complete with long sleeves (which she wore rolled up to her mid-forearms) paired with an intricate, totally sheer brown beaded maxiskirt. Charlize layered the high-waisted skirt over nothing but a pair of black low-rise underwear, and she accessorized the contrasting look with a black clutch, matching black sandals, and gold letter rings that spelled out “Dior.”

The A-lister rounded out her stylish ‘fit by pulling her blonde hair up into a slicked-back bun with a middle part — showing off her Dior pearl earrings in the process —and by adding a light pink eyeshadow, feathered lashes, and a glossy peach lip.

Theron’s Parisian outing comes amid a busy (and fashionable) month for the star, who was spotted in similar takes on work-meets-party wear when attending New York Fashion Week earlier this month.

On Sept. 7, the star stepped out for yet another Dior show, this time in the Big Apple, while wearing a summery navy blue slip dress layered under a chunky quarter-zip sweater. Adding even more confusion as to where, exactly, this outfit should be worn, Charlize added a stack of pearl necklaces and a white Dior clutch to accessorize the ensemble, and she finished it off with black sandals and a sleek bun.
