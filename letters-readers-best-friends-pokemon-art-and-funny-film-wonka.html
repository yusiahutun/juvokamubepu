Letters from readers: Best friends, Pokemon art and the funny film Wonka

Do you have something to share? Send us your letters using this form.

Hillary Pang Tsz-cheng (aged 7), St Rose of Lima’s School

I like to play with my friend Hayley during breaks at school. We tell jokes, dance together and sing Christmas songs in our classroom.

I have a lot of tests at school, and it makes me happy because I like to be busy. I love my school because I have made so many friends and met a lot of teachers.

Joey and Onsie Chien (aged 9 and 7), Bloom KKCA Academy

We want to tell you about our Pokémon scroll.

It has 119 Pokémon and is a surprising 2,190cm long. We have been working on it for two years.

Almost every Pokémon has a colourful background design and details. One of the most detailed Pokémon is Espeon. We designed it based on the alternate art Espeon V card from the Evolving Skies Pokémon trading card game set. The background design has bookshelves with books inside.

Every detail was drawn by hand. It started as a colouring activity but grew into a long, beautiful scroll. We are very proud of it!

Summer Lee Suet-ching (aged 9), Harrow International School Hong Kong

I recommend watching the film Wonka because you can learn the exciting story of Willy Wonka and how he started his chocolate factory.

The movie also tells the origin story of the Oompa Loompas. The most hilarious part is when Wonka and his friends pretend to be animals in a zoo. One even has to pretend to be an octopus!

Wonka and his friends were invincible together, and they could even send a giraffe named Abigail into a church to cause chaos. I hope you will watch this movie and enjoy it as much as I did!
