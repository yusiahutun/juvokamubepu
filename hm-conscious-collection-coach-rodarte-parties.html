    This week LA was the place to be for stylish fêtes. Tuesday night, H&M celebrated the launch of their latest eco-friendly collection for their "Conscious" line—bringing out big names like Sasha Lane, Sara Foster, Orlando Bloom, Natalia Vodianova and more. Thursday night, a similarly stylish crowd assembled again to celebrate the hot new collaboration between Coach and LA-based designers Laura and Kate Mulleavy of Rodarte Thursday night.
