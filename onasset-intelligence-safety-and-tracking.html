OnAsset Intelligence

They started before smartphones existed when most assets were being tracked manually with very little technology deployed to automate data collection. They were also the first company to have a real-time tracking device formally approved for use on passenger aircraft, and as a result, they were invited by the FAA to help establish the rules and regulations currently governing the use of tracking devices in the aviation industry.

OnAsset Intelligence is an end-to-end provider of supply chain tracking and asset visibility services using advanced sensor-based devices that monitor and protect high value and mission-critical shipments and assets. They have been in business for over a decade and their products are used in over 160 countries globally. They support customers needing real-time visibility, location and condition of high-value freight and other assets shipped across the globe via all transportation modes (air, ground, rail, ocean).

They are a full OEM, and they design, manufacture and maintain all of our hardware, software and service products including a premium line of wireless tracking/sensing devices, network connectivity services, and a global cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) operating platform for managing these assets efficiently over the web. They can execute quickly on specialized and unique requirements, and they serve hundreds of global customers including shippers, logistics providers, airlines, system integrators, technology providers and packaging customers moving high value and mission-critical goods including pharmaceuticals, perishables, medical devices, high-value electronics and much more.

Products/Services

OnAsset Intelligence work closely with their customers to understand their needs and sculpt their products to their requirements. It’s important to maintain a level of flexibility because no two customers are the same, everyone has unique needs and if you can’t engage and adapt it is difficult to truly succeed in this business.

SENTRY FlightSafe® tracking device/gateway, Sentinel sensor tags, OAInsight SaaS platform and mobile app, and product customization/integration services. They differentiate themselves from others with the most long-term experience deploying and supporting their solutions globally, they are the OEM of every aspect of our solutions, they hold 17 issued patents on the technologies embedded into their products, and they also hold more aviation/airline approvals than any other company in the market. 

OnAsset Intelligence have a wide range of products and services that are uniquely distinguished and provide effective and foolproof solutions –

  • VISION PLATFORM:

Vision™ is an enterprise-grade, software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that enables you to manage your mobile assets fast and effectively, with no software to install to get started. A complete supply chain Control Tower experience at your fingertips. Their latest iteration is built using HTML5 to provide a seamless user experience across a multitude of systems and devices.

  • ENTERPRISE API:

OnAsset Intelligence understand that you have existing IT systems. They know that a good tracking and monitoring solution must play nice with your current day-to-day operations. This is why they created their Enterprise API – to deliver your data to you in a secure manner leveraging industry-standard methods and formats to make it easy.

  • HYBRID SOLUTIONS:

Customized solutions leveraging our extensive portfolio of device designs and intellectual property. They’ve been in business for over a decade, and they’ve experienced the evolution of the industry first hand. 

From the start with RFID technology to our experimentation with Wi-Fi, UWB, LoRa, Satellite and cellular – they have an appreciation for all technologies. They’ve made a business out of integrating the right technologies into cohesive and scalable solutions, and we understand what it takes to design, launch, scale and support global products.

  • RFID Portals

RFID scanning systems and portals based on flexible designs to allow for rapid configuration adjustments to meet varying customer requirements. Ready to install portals built to accommodate both storefront applications as well as industrial environments. More flexible and less costly than other pre-packaged RFID portal solutions.

  • Crime Prevention Kit (CPK)

The CPK is a pre-packaged kit that includes devices, accessories and service with a handy carrying case. Used to provide quick-dispatch tracking and monitoring on the fly. Ideal for law enforcement and customers needing access to a small number of devices for quick deployment.

OnAsset have achieved an unparalleled stature in this industry and has earned a lot of respect from the competitors and the consumer base alike.

  • SmartContainer®

Boutique hybrid cases that combine a variety of radio and sensor technologies in a self-contained and reusable package. They’ve created SmartContainers that include cellular, RFID, sensors, cameras and electronic locks for unique evidence integrity and chain-of-custody management systems. Ranging from briefcase-size to full TEU container.

The Aim and Vision

The development of their patented FlightSafe® technology allowed them to become the first real-time tracking device formally approved by industry regulators and airlines for use on commercial aircraft. And this marked a new beginning for OnAsset Intelligence and the industry equally. After this achievement, there was no looking back for them. They put in everything in their arsenal to provide the best products and services, meeting the highest standards and expectations of the clients, consumers, etc.

They have achieved an unparalleled stature in this industry and has earned a lot of respect from the competitors and the consumer base alike. They have a very strong team which has led them to this achievement, and the team truly believes in customer enthusiasm, edge technology, intellectual property and product extensions that address evolving customer needs while focusing on utilizing this technology to enhance business velocity, offer new services, and automate supply chain processes. This not only pushes them to move forward but also inspire them to keep innovating and progressing without losing their aim.

They are not holding anything back and are pacing forward in top gear with a lot of additions to their arsenal. They are launching a new product (SENTRY 600 FlightSafe®) in Q1 2021, and an extension of that product called NexBox which is a fully self-tracking, self-securing and a self-delivering reusable box that can also be equipped with UV-C sterilization to offer contact less final mile delivery of its contents.
