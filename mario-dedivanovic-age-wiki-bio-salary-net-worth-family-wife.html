Mario Dedivanovic

The hand behind the glam look of the celebrities are their beauticians or make-up artist. We won’t be wrong if we call them the magicians who make each face beautiful by their magic brushes. Today we are also talking about a make-up artist who became the reason for the glam look of several celebrities especially, Kim Kardashian. He is non-other than the makeup pro and BFF of Kim, Mario Dedivanovic.

Mario is a make-up pro who started his career as a Sephora fragrance consultant. Now, he has become one of the best artists with an amazing net worth and salary. Know more about such a great personality from this biography:

Family of Mario Dedivanovic

Born on 1st October 1983 in New York City, New York under the birth sign Libra, Mario Dedivanovic is an American make-up artist. He holds both American and Albanian nationalities and belongs to Albanian descent.

Moreover, his Albanian immigrant parents raised him in the Bronx, New York with his sister, Marina Dedivanovic. The whole family started living in New York henceforth. Still, his family is very tight-knit. Further, his sister helped him to organize his master classes which show that the family is close to one another.

When it comes to Mario Dedivanovic’s professional life, he is a make-up artist. He started his makeup career as a Sephora employee. He joined Sephora as a fragrance consultant and eventually came out as a makeup pro. Mario explained,

”They put me by the door to say, ‘Welcome to Sephora!’ to every single person. Because I was near the door, there was like a lipstick gondola nearby, and women would ask questions. I just started giving them advice… even though I wasn’t supposed to. I quickly became obsessed with makeup and began to research and study it after work.”

Mario then worked for Fox News. Moreover, he collaborated with Cover Girl, Marc Jacobs, and Laura Mercier among others. He also appeared on the Lifetime TV series Glam. Despite several works, he is known as the make-up artist of the famous celebrity, Kim Kardashian.

Similarly, Mario has many other celebrity clients like Jennifer Lopez, Priyanka Chopra, Demi Lovato, Gabrielle Union, and Chrissy Teigen among others.

According to the Influencer Marketing Hub, Mario makes an earning of between $11,270-$18,783 per post on his Instagram profile titled makeupbymario which has over 5.6 million followers. Furthermore, Mario has a beauty line where he sells several beauty products. Besides, he owns a house in New York. According to various YouTube videos, he enjoys a huge net worth of $15 Million as of 2018.

Furthermore, Mario has also a YouTube channel where he has around 93K subscribers. He has uploaded the videos of his make up art and exploration of it. According to the Social Blade, Dedivanovic earns monthly between $2 – $30 and a yearly earning of between $22 – $359. We hope the number of subscribers will increase very soon.

Hence, Mario is living a luxurious life.

Age
