Sep 4, 2024 Reinaldo Massengill

LA Knight - Age, Family, Bio

An American professional wrestler. He is presently under contract with WWE, where he entertains audiences on the SmackDown brand using his ring name. He also gained recognition during his tenure in Impact Wrestling where he used the name Eli Drake. He stands at 6 feet 1 inch and weighs approximately 240 pounds. 

Before Fame

In 2003, he embarked on his wrestling journey on the independent circuit. In 2010, he became part of the Championship Wrestling from Hollywood, which was then affiliated with the NWA. 

Trivia

He appears as a playable character in WWE 2K22 and WWE 2K23. He has garnered more than 470,000 followers on his reallaknight Instagram account. 

Family Life

His real name is Shaun Ricker. He was born in Hagerstown, Maryland. He is the youngest of three children. 

Associated With

In 2013 he made notable appearances as a cast member on The Hero, a reality series hosted by Dwayne Johnson.
