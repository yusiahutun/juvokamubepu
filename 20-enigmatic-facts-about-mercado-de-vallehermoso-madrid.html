The Mercado de Vallehermoso has a rich history.

Founded in 1933, the Mercado de Vallehermoso has stood as a testament to Madrid’s vibrant culinary scene for nearly a century. Its origins can be traced back to the idea of creating a central market where locals could find fresh produce, seafood, meats, and an array of other products.

It is located in the Chamberí district of Madrid.

Situated in the heart of the Chamberí district, the Mercado de Vallehermoso is a bustling hub of activity. The neighborhood itself is known for its charming streets, beautiful architecture, and vibrant local culture.

The market offers a diverse range of products.

From fresh fruits and vegetables to gourmet cheeses, meats, and seafood, the Mercado de Vallehermoso has something to satisfy every culinary craving. It is a food lover’s paradise, providing both locals and visitors with a vast selection of high-quality ingredients.

It serves as a meeting point for locals.

The Mercado de Vallehermoso is not just a place to shop for groceries; it is also a social gathering spot. Locals often meet up with friends and neighbors to enjoy a coffee, a glass of wine, or a delicious meal at one of the market’s many eateries.

The market has undergone a recent renovation.

In 2018, the Mercado de Vallehermoso underwent an extensive renovation to modernize its facilities while preserving its historical charm. The renovation project aimed to create a more welcoming and attractive space for both vendors and visitors alike.

It features a “Mercado de los Niños” section.

One of the unique aspects of the Mercado de Vallehermoso is its dedicated area for children. Known as “Mercado de los Niños,” this section offers various activities and workshops for kids, promoting a love for fresh and healthy food from an early age.

The market hosts regular events and workshops.

The Mercado de Vallehermoso is not just a place to shop; it is also a center for cultural and gastronomic events. From cooking classes to wine tastings, the market offers a diverse range of activities that engage and entertain visitors.

It showcases local and organic products.

The Mercado de Vallehermoso is committed to promoting sustainable and locally sourced products. Visitors can find a wide variety of organic fruits and vegetables, artisanal cheeses, homemade preserves, and other environmentally friendly products.

The market has a vibrant atmosphere.

The market offers a variety of dining options.
