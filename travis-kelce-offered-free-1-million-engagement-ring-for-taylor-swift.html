Travis Kelce offered 'free $1 million engagement ring' for Taylor Swift

Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s engagement rumors are still the talk of the town, and now a jeweler wants to gift a ring to the NFL heartthrob for free.

Forget Swifties, it looks like the entire world is shipping Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, including jewelers. They’re hoping to hear about the ‘good news’ soon and are still on the edge of their seats. Amid rumors Travis Kelce may propose to Taylor in the upcoming off-season, a jeweler has now come forward to gift him a free ring to make it happen. The ring is worth a whopping $1 million.

Travis Kelce is offered a ‘free’ engagement ring

Philadelphia-based jewelry designer Steven Singer is ready to offer all the help he can give to Travis Kelce in finding the perfect engagement ring for Taylor Swift. The designer already has a custom-made ring in mind, which would be perfect for the Grammy-winning celebrity.

Talking to Page Six, Steven shared: “I recognize that the most renowned couple globally has an abundance of options when it comes to choosing engagement rings, should they decide to tie the knot.”

He added: “If they do, I would be honored to help design a truly extraordinary ring exclusively for them.”

The jeweler has his own ‘personal’ reason for wanting to help Travis and Taylor.

He shared: “My daughter is an avid fan of Taylor Swift, and we admire not only her but also the values she champions.”

So he believes, by helping Tay Tay and Travis, he may ultimately help secure the most important thing Swifties desire!

He joked: “Perhaps, by presenting them with the ring of their dreams, it might make securing concert tickets for her next tour a tad easier.”

The ‘engagement’ may be postponed

Insiders close to Travis Kelce and Taylor dished that the duo are spending time having deep conversations about their future.

The Miss Americana and Heartbreak Prince crooner has “only been dating (Travis Kelce) for a few months,” but “they’ve had plenty of really deep conversations about their future together,” the source offered.

But the real engagement may take longer than fans anticipated. NFL star Jake Funk’s wife Hannah Ann Sluss gives an idea of what the rumored timeline may look like.

The celebrity shares: “Usually, engagements, from what Jake’s told me, happen in the off-season.”

NFL’s off-season will only begin after March. However, Taylor is scheduled to be on her record-breaking Eras tour from February onwards. So fans who were waiting to hear about the engagement during Christmas break may need to wait a few more months.
