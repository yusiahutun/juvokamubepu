Shopping for gifts for any holiday or occasion is hard. When you’re searching for someone you’re really close to, there’s a lot of pressure to buy something personal. And when you’re shopping for someone you’re not sure about, it’s hard to determine what they’d actually like. But when all else fails, we’ve got your go-to covered: affordable jewelry gifts.

But of course, as with everything else, finding cute jewelry on a budget can seem impossible. Especially if you don’t want the gift receiver to know it was purchased on a budget. Scroll through below to shop our budget-friendly jewelry gift guide, of course. Ahead, you’ll find our fave giftable jewelry pieces at every price point—we promise your giftee will love them.

“When considering an affordable jewelry brand, customers should ensure the materials used align with the desired lifespan of the jewelry,” says David Schoenfeld, founder and CEO of Ring Bear. “If the goal is to wear the piece for an extended period, it’s worth investing in gold or platinum. If the piece is trend-based or seasonal, then gold vermeil or silver can be great options.”

If you are purchasing gold or sterling silver, “any reputable jeweler will include standardized markings on the interior of the piece,” adds Schoenfeld.

If you’re concerned about ethics, including diamond mining, etc. “customers should also ensure the brand aligns with their values. Do they act sustainably? Do they have positive reviews from previous customers? With this confirmation, customers can feel good knowing the jewelry represents themselves, regardless of price,” says Schoenfeld.
