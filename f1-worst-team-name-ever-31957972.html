'Worst team name in F1 history' confirmed along with a cringe bio to leave fans despairing

The sister team of the all-conquering Red Bull machine has undergone a rebrand – and F1 fans hate it.

A new multi-year global partnership has been born between Red Bull and Visa which has seen another change of name to the second team owned by Austrian engine drink conglomerate Red Bull - formerly known as Scuderia Toro Rosso and later to Red Bull’s AlphaTauri to promote Red Bull’s fashion brand.

AlphaTauri’s name change has been widely followed with the team looking set to be named ‘Racing Bulls’ in autumn last year. But that idea was dismissed for being too similar to Red Bull and the commercial and marketing team soon went to work on a new name.

And fans have been left furious after it was confirmed that AlphaTauri have been renamed Visa Cash App RB Formula One team with immediate effect. The rebrand will see the two sister teams on the track ramped up as much as current rules allow as they now share a marketing and communications department.

But fans couldn’t care about the financial gains that both teams are set to profit from, instead loathing the idea of the name and also their incredibly cringey bio: “A Formula 1 team working weekends always pure vibes.” One comment on Twitter said: “I know the elite levels of all sports are only truly concerned with financial gain but even by the current extremely low expectations, that’s hilarious.”
