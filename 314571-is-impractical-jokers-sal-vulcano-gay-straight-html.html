Is Sal Vulcano gay?

In 2014, Sal appeared on the Daytime talk show with Sally Jessy alongside his friends. Sal had a little secret that he wanted to share with his friends. He went ahead and said he was gay.

His friend James Murray also opened up, stating that he was gay too, crediting Sal as a source of inspiration and resilience.

Is Sal Vulcano actually gay?

After James' confession, Sal said that he was not gay. His claim to being gay was simply a ruse, motivated by his desire to help his friend James Murray in revealing his true sexual orientation. He stated:

It makes me feel great that my best friend James has come out of the closet. That is the reason I brought him here today. You see, I am not gay but I knew if I said I was gay, he would admit that he was gay.
