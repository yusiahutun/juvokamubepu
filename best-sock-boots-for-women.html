15 Best Sock Boots for Women 2023

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

A Burgundy Block-Heel Sock Boot

Nomasei Strada Burgundy Boots

A Burgundy Block-Heel Sock Boot

Nomasei Strada Burgundy Boots

These boots aren’t like the others—they’re cast in a deep burgundy (our favorite trending color), with a neutral stripe along the back zipper. And they’re set on a low, block heel for effortless strolling.

Heel height: 1.5"

Sizes: 6–12

Materials: 100% stretch lamb leather

Pliable and glovelike stretch Napa leather are the foundations of this popular sock boot from Reformation. Wear yours with wide-leg denim or side-split flare leggings for an evening look.

Sizes: 5–11

Materials: Napa leather

Cut into a graceful knee-high shape, these slender Toteme boots have sharp pointed toes and kitten heels for all-day wearing.

Sizes: 5–12

A Buttercream Sock Boot

Neous Ran Stretch-Leather Boot
