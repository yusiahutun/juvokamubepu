Macy Jenkins Career and Net Worth 

Macy Jenkins fell in love with journalism while studying for Master of Science in Broadcast and Digital Journalism at Syracuse University's Newhouse School of Public Communications. Before that, she mostly worked in the marketing field after completing a Bachelor's degree in Communication.

Jenkins' first journalism work was an Assignment Desk Intern at KABC-TV in Los Angeles. After that's, she served as Multimedia Journalist at NCC News and Washington Reporter at KOLR 10 Ozark's First.
