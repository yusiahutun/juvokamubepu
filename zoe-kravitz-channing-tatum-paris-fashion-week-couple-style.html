Zo Kravitz and Channing Tatum Win Paris Fashion Week's Couple Style

Zoë Kravitz and Channing Tatum didn’t hesitate to enjoy a PDA-filled outing during Paris Fashion Week.

The couple, who attended Saint Laurent’s Spring/Summer 2024 runway show last night, were photographed exiting the venue hand in hand with smiles on their faces. They cutely coordinated in all-black ensembles.

Kravitz looked fabulous in a micro minidress draped in layers of black mesh fabric adorned all over with large white polka dots. She capped off the look with a pair of glossy black pump slingbacks, pearl stud earrings, a long rectangular black clutch, and—for a pop of color—a bright red manicure. Outside, Kravitz stayed warm by layering a boxy black blazer on top.

Meanwhile, Tatum looked suave in a crisp black button-up layered on top of a white undershirt, plus black trousers.

Saint Laurent’s star-studded front row additionally included the likes of Austin Butler, Hailey Bieber, and Rosé from Blackpink.

Kravitz opened up about her romance with Tatum in a 2022 interview with GQ.

“He’s just a wonderful human,” she said at the time. “He makes me laugh and we both really love art and talking about art and the exploration of why we do what we do. We love to watch a film and break it down and talk about it and challenge each other.”

The two worked together on Kravitz’s forthcoming directorial debut, Pussy Island. She added that Tatum proved to be a supportive force during filming. “Whether it was making me tea or pouring me a drink or going to whip someone into shape or whatever—he really was my protector and it was really wonderful and sweet,” she said. “I think if you can do something like that together, it’s a good test. And we came out even stronger.”
