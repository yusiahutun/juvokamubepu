ITV Emmerdale beauty 'wants divorce' from husband 'who left her for co-star'

Actress Amy Nuttall has reportedly stated that she wants to divorce her husband Andrew Buchan after he allegedly left her for his co-star.

The Emmerdale beauty was said to have been left devastated after Broadchurch star Andrew reportedly left Amy days before Christmas for his Better co-star Leila Farzad.

Amy and Andrew have been married since 2012 and share two children together.
