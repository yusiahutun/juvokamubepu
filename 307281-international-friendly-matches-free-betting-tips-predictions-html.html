Fiji has won twice, drew one and lost one of their last four matches. They have scored in each of the four appearances and won their previous game against Mauritius in a fixture that ended 1-0. They are undisputedly the better team whenever they play at home. As they welcome Tahiti, it’s all systems go as they seek to extend their two-match unbeaten streak at home. Tahiti, on the other hand, lost their last match against Papua New Guinea in a home fixture and have cumulatively lost twice in their previous six games. The two sides are goal scoring machines, but this fixture is more likely to end in favour of Fiji.

On the other hand, Haiti may well be coming to this game a bit determined but not as competitive. Match prediction: Bermuda to win

The Korea Republic will once again be on the pitch to prove their worth as they host Australia in their next international friendly fixture. The home team have so far outmatched the likes of Columbia and Bolivia, but a more challenging game awaits them come Friday. The Socceroos will be matching into this game to make amends after a poor show in the 2019 Asian cup.

Match prediction: Australia to win

The Eagle Soft Carthage lost in their game against North African side Algeria in the 2019 warm-up matches and won’t be taking any chances this time around. On the other hand, the Asood Al-Rafidain are equally equipped ahead of this fixture.

Match prediction: Kenya to win

Ivory Coast has two remaining international friendlies before the AFCON competitions in Egypt. In their last friendly fixture, they outplayed Liberia and emerged with a 1-0 victory. On the other hand, Comoros displayed a great performance in the COSAFA Cup, edging out both Zimbabwe and Mauritius in the semi and quarter-finals respectively.
