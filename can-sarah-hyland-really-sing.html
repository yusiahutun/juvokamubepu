Can Sarah Hyland Really Sing?

Sarah Hyland has shown off her singing skills in public on multiple occasions, with one of the most notable times coming when she performed at the 2019 Teen Choice Awards. The actor turned singer took to the stage to debut the song "Met At A Party" alongside Jordan McGraw (who just so happens to be the son of none other than Dr. Phil!), per Entertainment Tonight, and it's safe to say that fans were impressed. Twitter was flooded with praise (and surprise!) as Hyland's voice was heard by a mainstream audience for one of the first times. "@Sarah_Hyland Sarah [you're] the most amazing singer dam girl," one person tweeted. "Right now i am watching the #2019 teen choice Awards and dam girl u and @Jorden killed it on stage," they added. Another shared their surprise, tweeting, "I didn't know Sarah Hyland can sing and is a singer."

But that actually wasn't the first time Hyland has shown off her powerful voice in a public way. Three years earlier, she wowed the world when she teamed up with the band Boyce Avenue to perform a stripped down cover of The Chainsmokers and Halsey's hit "Closer," which was posted to their YouTube.
