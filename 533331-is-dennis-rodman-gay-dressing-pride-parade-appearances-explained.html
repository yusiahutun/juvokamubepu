Who is Dennis Rodman?

Dennis Keith Rodman is an American former professional basketball player. He was born on May 13th, 1961, in Trenton, New Jersey, United States. He is the eldest son of Shirley and Philander Rodman, Jr.

Dennis started playing basketball in 1986 and was active until 2006. Over this period, he has played for various NBA teams, including Detroit Pistons, San Antonio Spurs, Chicago Bulls, and Los Angeles Lakers.

Rodman is renowned for his tenacious defensive skills, vibrant hair, and remarkable career. He won several team and individual awards. He secured five NBA championships, and his legacy led to an induction into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame in 2011.
